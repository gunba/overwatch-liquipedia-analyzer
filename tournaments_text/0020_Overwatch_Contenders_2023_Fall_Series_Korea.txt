==Information==
===Format===
*Invited
**Bottom 5 {{LeagueIconSmall/owl}} [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Summer Stage/Knockouts|Summer Stage Knockouts]] teams, top 5 teams from {{LeagueIconSmall/wdg}} [[WDG/Overwatch Open Tournament/Korea/2023/Fall|WDG Open Fall]] are invited
* Group Stage
** Each Group is a 5-team Group.
** Round Robin
** Top 3 teams of each group advance to Playoffs.
***<span class="bg-seedup">Top team</span> advances to [[Overwatch Contenders/2023/Fall Series/Korea|Playoffs]] Semifinals.
***<span class="bg-up">Each Group 2nd 3rd</span> advances to [[Overwatch Contenders/2023/Fall Series/Korea|Playoffs]] Quarterfinals.
* Playoffs
**'''single Elimination'''
