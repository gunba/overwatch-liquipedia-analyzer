==Information==
===Format===
*Invited
**Bottom 2 {{LeagueIconSmall/owl}} [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Opens|Spring Stage Opens]] teams, top 6 teams from {{LeagueIconSmall/wdg}} [[WDG/Overwatch Open Tournament/Korea/2023/Summer|WDG Open]] are invited
* Group Stage
** Each Group is a 4-team Group.
** Round Robin
**  Top 3 teams of each group advance to Last Chance Qualifier.
* Last Chance Qualifier
** single Elimination
*** The winner between the first-placed teams of each group becomes the 1st seed,the other becomes the 2nd seed.
*** The winner between the second-placed teams of each group becomes the 3rd seed,the other becomes the 4th seed.
*** The winner between the third-placed teams of each group becomes the 5th seed,the other is eliminated.
** Top 5 teams advance to {{LeagueIconSmall/owl}} [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Summer Stage/Opens|Summer Stage Opens]]
