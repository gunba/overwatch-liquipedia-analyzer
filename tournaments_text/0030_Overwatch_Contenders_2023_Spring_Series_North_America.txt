===Format===
*'''Group Stage:'''

**<span class="bg-down">Bottom 4</span> teams drop to {{LeagueIconSmall/open division}} [[Open_Division/2023/Summer_Qualifier/North_America|Open Division Playoffs]]

**Double-elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}
**All other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
