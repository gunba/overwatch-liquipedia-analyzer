==Information==
===Format===
*A-Sides
**64-team single-elimination to determine top 8 teams
**8-team double-elimination bracket
<!--**Top 8 teams from [[Overwatch Contenders/2021/Season 3/Korea|2021 Season 3]] are invited as top seeds
**Top 32 teams from [[Open Division/2022/Season 1/Korea|Open Division]] get early registration-->
*B-Sides
**12-team double-elimination to determine top 4 teams
**Top4 teams advance to Group Stage
**A-Sides Top4 teams invited Group Stage
**Group Stage Top6 teams advance to double-elimination playoffs
