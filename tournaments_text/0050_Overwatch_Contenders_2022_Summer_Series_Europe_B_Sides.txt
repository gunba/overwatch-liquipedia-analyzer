==Information==
===Format===
*A-Sides
**64-team single-elimination to determine top 8 teams
**8-team double-elimination bracket
**Top 8 teams from [[Overwatch Contenders/2021/Season 3/Europe|2021 Season 3]] are invited as top seeds
**Top 32 teams from [[Open Division/2022/Season 1/Europe|Open Division]] get early registration
*B-Sides
**All 2021 Contenders teams and top 4 non-2021 Contenders from A-Sides qualify
**12-team round-robin to determine top 8 teams
**8-team double-elimination playoff bracket
