==Controversy==
*During the Upper Bracket Finals between [[01_Esports]] and [[Munich eSports]], the match was halted while Munich eSports were leading 3-2 as Blizzard admins had realised the match should have been {{Abbr/Ft3}} instead of {{Abbr/Ft4}}. Therefore, Munich eSports was adjudged to have won the match 3-0 and progressed to the Grand Finals. <br>
*In the next match, the Lower Bracket Finals between 01_Esports and [[Ex Oblivione]], both teams refused to play the game out, with the Ex Oblivione organisation tweeting out they would "refuse to play out the series until the issues have been addressed".<ref name="ExO Statement">{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/ExOblivioneOW/status/1552751395547369472|title=Ex Oblivione Official Statement|publisher=[[Ex Oblivione]]|date=2022-07-28}}. </ref><br>
*Following on, [[kraandop]] of 01_Esports tweeted out that the rules on the bracket platform Gamebattles had it as {{Abbr/Ft4}} as well as the fact that both teams had clarified with tournament admins the day before that the series would be {{Abbr/Ft4}} to which the admins confirmed.<ref name="kraandop b07"> {{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/kraandopOW/status/1552743316915109888|title=We were told it was best of 7 A DAY before the match and both teams re-checked this fact. We asked the admins and they came back to us saying this was in fact correct.|publisher=[[kraandop]]|date=2022-07-28}}</ref> This further spawned outrage throughout the community against Blizzard admins with the #ContendersStrike hashtag trending as a result.<ref name="contendersstrike">{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/jakesucky/status/1552760522528854016|title=The Overwatch Contenders League is on strike and trending|publisher=Jake Lucky|date=2022-07-28}}</ref><br>
*Less than an hour before the North American matches were due to start, the official Contenders Path to Pro Twitter account tweeted out a statement, clarifying that official tournament rules did say that it was a {{Abbr/Ft3}} series, but admitted that the fairest course of action would be to maintain consistency with their latest communication with the teams. The match between 01_Esports and Munich eSports would continue the following day with the latter leading 3-2.<ref name="official contenders statement">{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/owpathtopro/status/1552773819256823808|title=Contenders Statement|publisher=owpathtopro|date=2022-07-28}}</ref>
