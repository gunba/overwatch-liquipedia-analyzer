==Information==
===Format===
*Each team must have at least 3 Saudi players in the starting line-up.
* '''Group Stage:'''
** {{Abbr/Bo5}} Round-Robin format
** Top four teams advance to the '''Playoffs'''

* '''Playoffs:'''
** {{Abbr/Bo5}} elimnation bracket
** 1st and 2nd teams play in the upper bracket, while 3rd and 4th teams play in lower bracket.
