'''Mrs. Catriarchy's Charity Event''' is a charity tournament organized by [https://twitter.com/bame1twitch bame1]. Proceeds from the stream will be donated to [https://translifeline.org Trans Lifeline].

==Information==
* Three Contenders teams and a streamer team was invited.
* Single elimination bracket
* First to 2 semifinals 
* First to 3 finals
* All maps are allowed
* First map is pre-determined. Loser picks next map, winner picks side.
