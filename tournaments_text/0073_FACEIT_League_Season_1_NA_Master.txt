==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''April 30<sup>th</sup> - June 9<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Round Robin format
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 8 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 14<sup>th</sup> - June 23<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Double elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}, all other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 3 teams}} qualify for {{LeagueIconSmall/esports world cup}} [[Esports World Cup/2024|Esports World Cup 2024]]

===Relegation===
*{{Bgcolortext|stay|1st-12th teams}} remain in Master division for {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league master}} [[FACEIT_League/Season_2/NA/Master|Season 2]]
*{{Bgcolortext|staydown|13th-14th place teams}} play in {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 1/NA/Relegation|Relegation Matches]]
*{{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 2 teams}} are relegated to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/NA/Expert|Season 2 Expert]]
