==Information==

===Format===
* '''Trials''': Single Elimination and Double Elimination
** '''Round 1''': Single Elimination
*** Round 1 are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
*** Round 1 Top (2) teams advance to the Round 2.
** '''Round 2''': Double Elimination
*** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
* '''Contenders''': Group Stage
** '''Group Stage''': Round Robin
*** Group Stage are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
*** Group Stage Top (8) teams advance to the inter league.
** '''inter league''': 
*** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
*** Playoffs after a ranking match Top (7)
** '''Playoffs''': King of the Hill
