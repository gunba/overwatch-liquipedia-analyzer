===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**All matches are '''first-to-3'''.
**<span class="bg-up">'''Top two (2)'''</span> teams of each group advance to Playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Semifinals are '''first-to-3''' and Finals are '''first-to-4'''.
