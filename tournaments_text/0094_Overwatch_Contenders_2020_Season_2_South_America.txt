==Information==
*The 8 teams that qualified for the Season 1 Playoffs will automatically qualify for Season 2. Along with 2 new teams who have passed the qualifying stages: Open Division and Trials.
*These 10 teams will face each other during the second semester in a circuit of 4 league tournaments in groups where they will accumulate points, prizes and champion titles.
*The top 8 teams of the Regular Season qualify for Playoffs

===Format===
*'''Regular Season'''
**Series of 10-team round robins (two groups of five teams).
**Followed by a brief single-elimination tournament.
*'''Playoffs'''
**The top 8 teams of the Regular Season qualify for Playoffs
