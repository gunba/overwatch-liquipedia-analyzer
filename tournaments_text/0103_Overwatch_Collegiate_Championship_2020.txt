==Information==
===Format===
*Varsity Invitational
**Two groups of eight teams will compete in a Round Robin format within their perspective group
*Varsity Qualifiers
**Teams that are verified by Tournament Administration as eligible varsity programs will be separated into groups of up to 8.
**During the Main Season time frame, teams will compete in a Round Robin format within each group
*Club Qualifiers
**Club teams will be separated into groups of up to 12.
**Teams will compete in a standard Swiss format throughout the Main Season time frame
*National Championship
**64-team single-elimination tournament
