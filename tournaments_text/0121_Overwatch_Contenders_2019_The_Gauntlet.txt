==Information==
*The Gauntlet will consist of 10 teams competing in group stages and ultimately a double-elimination bracket. 
*Top-performing regions will send their teams from Season 2 to the Gauntlet, and performance in the Pacific and Atlantic Showdowns will determine region-specific invites.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage:''' (Double Elimination)
**Eight of the ten teams are split in two '''groups of four'''.
***Champions of North America (as decided by a [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/The_Gauntlet_Tiebreaker|tiebreaker]]) and Korea will bypass Group Stage.
**Each group is a double elimination bracket.
**<span class="bg-up">Top three (3) teams</span> of each group advance to Finals.
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom team</span> of each group is eliminated.
**All matches are first-to-3.
*'''Finals:''' (Double Elimination)
**'''8 teams''' (champions of North America and Korea, as well as top three of each group) in a double elimination bracket.
**All matches are first-to-3, except for winners' finals, losers' finals and Gauntlet finals, which are first-to-4.
