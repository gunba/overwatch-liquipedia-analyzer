==Format==
*'''Preseason''' - ''September 23rd 2018 - December 9th 2018'' :
**Teams will play 12 weeks of matches, 2x a week to grant additional seeding toward the National Championship
<br>
*'''Group stage'''
**'''National League''' :
***Teams will compete at the national level in 7 rounds of swiss to determine their national placement.
**'''Regional League''' :
***Teams will be split into 16 different regions across North America, where after 5 weeks of Swiss and a single elimination bracket, the top 2 teams from each region will qualify for the National Championship.
**'''Varsity League''' :
***Recognized varsity schools from across North America will play regional sub-division matches to determine who qualifies for the National Championship. Each victor from all 4 regions qualifies.
<br>
*'''Playoffs'''
**'''National Championship''' :
***The top 64 teams battle it out in an epic single elimination bracket, seeding based on their performance from the preseason up until this point.
***The top eight teams travel to Houston, Texas, where the finals rounds are broadcast on [[wikipedia:ESPN|ESPN]] as part of the [https://twitter.com/CECESPN ESPN Collegiate Esports Championship].
