===Format===
*'''Group Stage'''
**Single round robin.
**Each match is a best of 4.
**Tiebreakers:
***Matches tiebreaker play in [[Ilios]].
*'''Playoffs'''
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**Finals matches are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
