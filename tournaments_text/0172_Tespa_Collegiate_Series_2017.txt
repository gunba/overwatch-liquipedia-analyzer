==About==

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''' - ''September 25th - November 12th''
**'''Regional Group Swiss''' - ''September 25th - November 5th'':
***The Regional Group Swiss will consist of ten (10) ''best-of-three'' matches that take place over a period of six (6) weeks. Teams will play against teams from their region (North, East, South, West) while fighting for a spot in the top ten (10) teams. Teams that have the same ending record as teams that are in the top ten (10) will also advance to the National Group Stage.
****Week 1 - ''September 25th - October 1st''
****Week 2 - ''October 2nd - October 8th''
****Week 3 - ''October 9th - October 15th''
****Week 4 - ''October 16th - October 22nd''
****Week 5 - ''October 23rd - October 29th''
****Week 6 - ''October 30th - November 5th''
**'''National Group Swiss''' - ''November 11th - November 12th''
***The top ten (10) teams from each region (including teams that tied with top ten (10) teams) will advance into a group swiss bracket with the other four regions. Records from your regional group stage matches remain as you play matches in the national group swiss. You will play four matches over a two day period. The '''top 40''' teams, based on their record from both the Regional Swiss and National Swiss, will advance to the elimination stages, regardless of region.
<br>
[[File:Tespa Elimination Stage.png|thumbnail|Elimination stage format]]
*'''Elimination Stage''' - ''November 15th, 2017 - February 17, 2018''
**'''Towers of Doom''' - ''November 15th - December 2nd''
***The Towers of Doom bracket is a single elimination bracket in which teams are placed deeper into the bracket based on their seeding from the Group Stages. Therefore, lower seeded teams will face more elimination matches of increasing difficulty while facing top seeded teams at the end of the bracket for a chance at the National Live Finals. Towers of Doom play will occur over two weeks and consist of four ''best-of-5 matches''.<br>'''Top 8 advance'''.
****Week 1 - ''November 15th - November 16th''
****Week 2 - ''November 29th - December 2nd''
**'''National Finals''' - ''December 3rd, 2017 - February 17, 2018''
***Quarterfinals (Top 8) - ''December 3rd, 2017''
***Finals (Top 4) - ''February 17, 2018''
