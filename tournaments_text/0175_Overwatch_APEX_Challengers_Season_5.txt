==About==
===Format===
*'''First Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**3 points awarded for a victory.
**0 points awarded for a defeat.
**'''Top 6''' teams advance to '''Second Group Stage'''.

*'''Second Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**3 points awarded for a victory.
**0 points awarded for a defeat.
**'''Top 4''' teams qualify for [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_5/Promotion|Overwatch APEX Season 5: Promotion Tournament]].

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).
