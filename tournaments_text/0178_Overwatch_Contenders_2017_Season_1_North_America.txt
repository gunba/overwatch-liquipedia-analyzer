==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''': Round Robin 
**All matches are '''4 Game Sets'''.
***If a series ends in a draw, a '''tiebreaker''' will be played.
**'''Standings Tiebreakers''' will first be total map wins.
***If teams that are tied have the same number of map wins at the end of the season, a final '''tiebreaker''' {{Abbr/Bo3}} [[Ilios]] will be played.
**'''Top 4''' teams at the end of the '''Regular Season''' advance to the Offline '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''':
**Playoffs take place at '''Blizzard Arena Los Angeles''' <ref name="Playoffs Location">[https://www.overwatchcontenders.com/en/news/article/59b04bdd484b080d940d599a JOIN US AT BLIZZARD ARENA LOS ANGELES ON OCTOBER 7–8]</ref>
**Playoffs are '''Single Elimination'''
**{{Abbr/Bo5}} Semi Finals
**{{Abbr/Bo7}} Grand Finals
