==Information==
'''Overwatch TaKeOver 2''' is the second ''Overwatch'' tournament by [[TaKeTV]], a production company well known for their ''StarCraft II'', ''Hearthstone'' and ''Counter-Strike: Global Offensive'' tournaments. 

TaKeOver features 8 teams from all over the world.

===Format===
*'''Groups''' - Round Robin.
**'''Top 2''' seeded to Upper Bracket
**'''3rd''' in Group seeded to Lower Bracket
**Group games are a '''4 Game Set'''.
***Teams winning the most maps move onto the Playoffs.
***If teams are tied on map score, it will be broken by head-to-head results and additional tiebreaker matches.
*'''Playoffs''' - Double Elimination.

===Map Pool===

***[[Eichenwalde]] / [[Numbani]] / [[Hollywood]]

**[[Control]]

===Map Rotation===
*'''4 Game Set''':
**1st Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Control]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.

*'''Tiebreaker'''
**{{Abbr/Bo1}} on [[Oasis]]

*'''Best of 3''':
**1st Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Control]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.

*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
**5th Set: [[Control]] map.
