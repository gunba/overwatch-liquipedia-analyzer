==About==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**3 points awarded for a 3:0 victory.
**2 points awarded for a 3:1 victory.
**1 point awarded for a 3:2 victory.
**0 points awarded for a defeat.

*Promotion Rules
**'''Top 6''' teams qualify for [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_4|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 4]].
***'''Top 4''' teams will also qualify for [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_3/Promotion|Overwatch APEX Season 3: Promotion Tournament]].
**'''Bottom 6''' teams are seeded in the [[Overwatch APEX/Season 2/Offline Qualifier/2|Overwatch APEX Season 2: Offline Qualifier #2]].

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).
