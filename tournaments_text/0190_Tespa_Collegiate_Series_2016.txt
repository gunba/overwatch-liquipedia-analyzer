==About==
===Format===
*'''Regional Group Stage''' - ''October 5th - November 10th, 2016:''
***The Regional Group Swiss will consist of five (5) matches that take place over a period of five (5) weeks. Teams will play against teams from their region (North, East, South, West). Teams with a winning record of 3-2 or higher will advance to the Regional Playoffs.
*'''Regional Playoffs''' - ''November 12th - November 20th, 2016:''
***ALl teams with a winning record play a single-elimination tournament against teams from their own region. The winner of each regional tournament advance to the National Championship.
*'''National Championship''' - ''February 19th, 2017:''
***The top four (4) teams compete in San Diego, California, in a single-elimination tournament to determine the National Champions.
