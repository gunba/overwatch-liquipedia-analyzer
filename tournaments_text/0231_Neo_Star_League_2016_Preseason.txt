===Format===
*'''Genaral Rules''': Competitive Mode
**Rematch in control maps if draw.
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**2 groups for 8 teams.
**All matches are Bo3.
**Top 2 of each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**All matches are Bo3 except Finals which is Bo5.
**Winner from Winners' Bracket takes advantage of 1:0 in Finals.
