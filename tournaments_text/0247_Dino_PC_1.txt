==Information==

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': 
**Best-of-1 Round Robin format
**32 teams divided into 4 groups
**2 teams from each group advance

*'''Playoffs''':
**8 teams
**Double Elimination
**All matches are Bo3.

*'''Final''' :
**The Grand Final from the playoffs
**Played at the DinoPC HQ in London
**Best-of-5 match
