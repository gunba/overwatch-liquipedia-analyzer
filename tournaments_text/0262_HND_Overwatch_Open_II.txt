==Overview==
* '''Finals''' will be a double elimination bracket held from September 30th through October 2nd. There will be 16 total spots, with 8 filled by the winners of the qualifiers and the other 8 filled by the teams with top average competitive rank. The teams with the top 8 average ranks do not need to compete in the qualifiers.
