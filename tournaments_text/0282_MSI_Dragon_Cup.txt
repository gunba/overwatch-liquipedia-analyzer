==Overview==
'''MSI Dragon Cup 6v6''' is the second LAN Overwatch tournament hosted in Malaysia.  The event was organized by '''M8 Gaming''' alongside MSI.  It was held at Wangsa Walk during the Asashi Gaming Expo 2016.

===Format===
*Double Elimination Bracket
**All matches {{Abbr/Bo3}},  grand finals {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Each team Attacks and Defends, the quickest time wins
