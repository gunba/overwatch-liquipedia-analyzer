==About==
===Format===
*Group Stages Stage 1 
**4x4 team Round Robin 
**Top team qualifies for the playoffs and 2-3 place advance to Group Stage 2 
*Group Stages Stage 2 
**2x4 team Double Elimination (GSL) Groups
**Top 2 of both groups advance to playoffs Playoffs 
**8 teams 
**Double Elimination 
**Best-of-3 bracket 
**Grand Final is a Best-of-9
*Playoffs 
**8 teams 
**Double Elimination 
**Best-of-3 bracket 
**Grand Final is a Best-of-9
