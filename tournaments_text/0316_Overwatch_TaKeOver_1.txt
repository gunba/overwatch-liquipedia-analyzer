'''TaKeOver''' is the first ''Overwatch'' tournament by [[TaKeTV]], a production company well known for their ''StarCraft II'', ''Hearthstone'' and ''Counter-Strike: Global Offensive'' tournaments. It is also the very first offline tournament after the official game release.
TaKeOver features 8 teams from all over the world.
