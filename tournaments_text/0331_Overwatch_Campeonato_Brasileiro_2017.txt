==About==
===Format===

*'''Group Stage''': Double Roun Robin
** All matches are 2 Game Sets.
***3 points awarded for a victory ('''2'''-0 or 0-'''2''').
***1 point awarded for a draw ('''1'''-'''1''').
***0 points awarded for a defeat ('''0'''-2 or 2-'''0''').
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**After the semifinals, for the definition of '''3rd and 4th places''', the following criteria will be applied, in that order: '''1)''' Most games won in the semifinal; and '''2)''' Final placement in the first phase (after playoffs).
