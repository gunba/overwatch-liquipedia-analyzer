==About==

===Location===
*Grail Quest 2017 was held at Turku Gatorade Center.

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Group Stage''': Double Elimination (''GSL'' format).
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group advance to playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination.
**Semifinals and 3rd position are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
