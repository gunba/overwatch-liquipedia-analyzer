==Overview==
'''SwitzerLAN''' is an annual lan tournament organized by SwitzerLAN.

==Information==
===Format===
*Round Robin group stage to determine seeding
*Double Elimination Bracket
*First Round and Lower Bracket Rounds are {{Abbr/Ft2}}, Upper Bracket Rounds and Lower Bracket Final are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
