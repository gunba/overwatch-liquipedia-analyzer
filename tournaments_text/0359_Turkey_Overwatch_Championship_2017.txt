===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Double Round Robin
**Eight (8) groups of three (3) teams.
**'''Top two''' (2) of each group advance to ''Playoffs''.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
