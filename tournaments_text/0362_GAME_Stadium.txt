==Information==
'''GAME Stadium''' is a [[:Category:Europe|Spanish]] league.
===Location===
*Cines Kinépolis de la Ciudad de la Imagen de Pozuelo en Alarcón, Madrid

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Regular Season''': Round Robin.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top 4''' advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
