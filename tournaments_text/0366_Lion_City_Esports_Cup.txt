==About==
The Lion City Esports Cup is an Esports tournament in Singapore, organised by SCOGA and Esports Academy; powered by the National Youth Council of Singapore; working hand in hand with partner Twitch and sponsors ChallengeMe.GG, Samsung, Aftershock and Secretlab; and produced by Zenway Productions Pte Ltd.

The Grand Finals be played at SHINE Festival 2017 that is held on 30th June 2017 - 2nd July 2017
===Format===
'''Online Qualifiers:''' Best of 3 Double Elimination.<br/>
'''Grand Finals:''' Best of 5, Winner Bracket team will have a 1 game advantage.
===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 3''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Escort]]/[[Hybrid]] map.

*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: Map advantage to winner bracket team.
**2nd Set: [[Control]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Escort]] map.
**4th Set: [[Assault]] map.
**5th Set: [[Escort]]/[[Hybrid]] map (excluding map chosen in 3rd set).

*In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo1}} round will be played on a [[Control]] map.
