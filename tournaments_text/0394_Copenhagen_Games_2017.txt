==About==
'''Copenhagen Games''' is a BYOC LAN tournament featuring players and teams from around the world. As usual, the event will take place at Ã˜ksnehallen in the heart of Copenhagen. The tournament is held by SLAP in cooperation with the Copenhagen Multiplicity.

Tournaments in ''League of Legends'', ''[[:counterstrike:Counter-Strike: Global Offensive|Counter-Strike: Global Offensive]]'', ''[[:Hearthstone:Hearthstone|Hearthstone]]'', ''FIFA'' and for the first time ''[[Overwatch]]'' will be played in Copenhagen.
