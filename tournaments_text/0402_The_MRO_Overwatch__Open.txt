==About==
===Format===
*'''Online Qualification Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**3 points awarded for a victory.
**0 points awarded for a defeat.
**If two teams are tied on points, it will be broken as follows:
***Result of head to head game.
***Total Maps won
***Maps difference: Total net score for maps played. Net score = maps gained, less maps gained against.
**If two or more teams are still tied, a random king of the hill map ({{Abbr/Bo5}}) will be chosen by the admins to determine the winner.     
**'''Top 8''' teams advance to the playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''': Double-elimination
**Winner's Bracket will be played online, Loser's Bracket will be played offline in-front of a live audience held during The Malta Robotics Olympiad.
**Playoff matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**Grand Final will also be played offline in {{Abbr/Bo7}} format.
**The team from the Winner's Bracket will start with a 1-0 lead.
