==Format==
*Round 1 and 2 –  Best of 1
 
Map Draft
Control Map* (Oasis,Iliois, Lijang Tower, Nepal) *Played best of 5 points
 
*Semi Finals onwards – Best of 3
Map Draft Pools
 
Map 1: Control Map** (Oasis,Iliois, Lijang Tower, Nepal) **Played best of 3 points
 
Map 2: Hybrid (Echenwalde, Hollywood, King’sRow, Numbani)
 
Map 3: Escort (Dorado, Route 66, Watchpoint: Gibraltar)
Higher seed drafts first. Lower seed chooses defense or attack first on Hybrid/Escort maps.
