==Information==
* After the tournament had been played, Tournament Administration was alerted to the fact that 'ineligible players' were playing on the [[INTerstella]] team.  Therefore the [[INTerstella]] team was disqualified.  This changed the final standings, and therefore [[PIXL eSports]] qualified instead of [[Surge eSports Club]].<ref name="Standings update">{{cite web|url=https://bnetcmsus-a.akamaihd.net/cms/bnet_header/pd/PD2D93KIHB111530246626905.pdf|title=Changes to the final Contender Australia Trials team standings have been updated for Season 2|author=Blizzard Esports ANZ|date=2018-06-29}}</ref>
