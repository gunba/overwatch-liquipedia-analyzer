==About==
'''Gamers Assembly 2019''' is the fourth installment of a [[:category:France|French]] LAN, sponsored by Omen by HP.<br>
On April eigth, the number of slots was reduced to 25<ref name="Dernier reslotage">{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/GamersAssembly/status/1115313359291736064|title=Dernière réorganisation des tournois et slots de la #GA2019}}</br>
{{cite web|url=https://ga2019.gamers-assembly.net/news/dernier-reslotage|title=Le nombre d'équipes Overwatch PC diminue à 25 (-7)|</br>|publisher='''GamersAssembly'''|date=2019-04-08}}</ref>. Later it is finally reduced to 20.

===Format===
* '''Groups''': 4 round-robin groups (5 teams)
** All matches played in {{Abbr/Bo1}}.
** First four teams in each group advance to playoffs.
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination bracket
** All bracket rounds: {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Winner & Loser Finals, Grand Finals: {{Abbr/Bo5}}.

* '''Open Division''': a few hours are reserved on saturday & sunday for teams to complete their Open Division matches.

===Broadcast talent===
* [[Zaroide]] reveals that the broadcasting of the tournament is provided by Omen by HP, with a team made of casters from the [[Bro'Casters]] & [[CastersNest]].
* A secondary stage follows the [[orKs Grand Poitiers]] team, represented by [[Cry me a River]]. The broadcast is handled by [[CastersNest]].
