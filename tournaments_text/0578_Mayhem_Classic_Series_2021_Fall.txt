==Information==
===Format===
*8 teams in a '''Double Elimination''' format.
*Matches will be played in a '''First to 2''' format.
*Grand Finals will be played in a '''First to 3''' format.
<!--
*Grand Finals will be played in a '''First to 4''' format.
*First map is picked by the higher seeded team.
*Every next map is picked by the team that has most recently lost a map.
*The team that does not pick the map chooses the side to start on.
*Map order:
**[[Control]] → [[Escort]] → [[Hybrid]] → start over.
**Semifinals and Grand Finals: [[Control]] → [[Escort]] → [[Hybrid]] → [[Assault]] → start over.
*The team in the Grand Finals which did not drop to the Losers Bracket will start Grand Finals with a 1-0 score.
