The '''HUE Invitational 2021''' event is an invitational hosted by Harrisburg University. It is a 2-day tournament with 16 collegiate teams.

==Format==
* Four four-team groups to determine top eight teams
* Eight-team single elimination bracket
* All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
