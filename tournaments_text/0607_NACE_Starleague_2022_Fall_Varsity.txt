'''NACE Starleague''' is a collegiate tournament organized by NACE and CSL Esports.

==Information==

===Format===
*Competitive Play ruleset
*Open only to NACE Members
*Premier division is comprised of the top varsity teams by skill level

*Regular Season:
**Teams split into regional conferences
**Seven-week round-robin
**Top team in each conference qualifies for playoffs
**Two teams who finished in second place in their conference qualify for playoffs
**Other second and third-place teams qualify for [[/Last Chance Qualifier|Last Chance Qualifier]]
*Playoffs:
**Single-elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are held in Philadelphia
