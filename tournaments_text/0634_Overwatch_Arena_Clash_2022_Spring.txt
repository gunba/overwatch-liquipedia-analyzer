'''Overwatch Arena Clash''' is a tournament organized by BELONG Gaming Arenas.

==Information==
===Format===
*Teams will play on location from Belong Arena locations against teams competing at other locations.
*Grand Finals are held at [[Insomnia/68|Insomnia68]] in Birmingham.
*'''Group Stage''': 
**All matches are Ft3
**Five swiss rounds to determine top six teams
*'''Playoffs''':
**Single elimination
**All matches are Ft3, Grand Finals are Ft4
