==Information==
===Format===
*Open only to NACE Members
*Premier division is comprised of the top varsity teams by skill level
*Regular Season:
**Teams split into regional conferences
**Seven-week round-robin
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top team}} in each conference qualifies for playoffs
**{{Bgcolortext|stayup|Second and third-place teams}} qualify for Last Chance Qualifier
*Playoffs:
**Single Elimination bracket
