==Overview==
* '''Group stage''' will be run in a round robin format on December 9th. Each team in a group must play one another in a BO1 format, the top two teams with the most wins in their group will move to the playoffs.
* '''Playoffs''' will be a double elimination bracket held on December 10th. There will be 8 total spots, filled by the top two teams of each Round Robin group. All games will be BO3, Grand Finals will be BO5.
