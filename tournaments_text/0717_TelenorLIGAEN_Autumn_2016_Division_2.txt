'''TelenorLIGAEN''' is an [[:Category:Norway|Norwagian]] Overwatch league. Minimum 4/6 players has to be [[:Category:Norway|Norwagian]] citizens. Each season lasts half a year.

==About==
===Format===
*'''League Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are Bo4.
**'''Top 2''' advance to '''Division 1'''
**'''Bottom 2''' is relegated to '''Division 3'''.
