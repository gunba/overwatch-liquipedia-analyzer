==Overview==
* '''Qualifiers''' will be done in a four group round robin format. Each team in a group must play one another in a BO1 format, the team with the most wins in their group will move to the Finals Bracket. Qualifiers will happen
* '''Finals''' will be a double elimination bracket held all day Saturday, November 12th. There will be 8 total spots, with 4 filled by the winners of the qualifiers and the other 4 filled by the highest seeded teams. The top 4 teams will not need to compete in the qualifiers.
