==Information==
'''SnasCUP''' is a [[:Category:Europe|Spanish]] tournament for amateur teams.

===Location===
*'''Red Computer''': Carrer de Sepúlveda, No.178, 08011 Barcelona, Spain.

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode.
* '''Playoffs''': Single elimination.
**The four seeds where given according to the '''SnasCUPs points''' earnd through the monthly tournaments ([[SnasCUP/7|7]], [[SnasCUP/8|8]] and [[SnasCUP/9|9]]).
