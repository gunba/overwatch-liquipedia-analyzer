==About==
===Format===

*'''Regular Season''': Double Round Robin
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Bottom 2 teams are relegated to the [[Bi-Monthly_Brawl/Season 2/Promotion|Promotion Tournament]]
*'''Playoffs''': Crucible
**First two matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}; Grand Final is {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
**The 3rd and 4th placed teams will play first, with the winner moving on the play against the 2nd placed team. The winner of the 2nd match will move on to play against the 1st placed team in the Grand Final.

*'''Map Choices''': The team defined as Team A will select the first map. The loser of this map will choose the next map and for each subsequent match, the loser will choose the next map from the appropriate map pool. Once a map is selected, it is eliminated from the map pool for the remainder of the match.
