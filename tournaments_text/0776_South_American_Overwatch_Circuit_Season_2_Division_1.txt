==Information==
The '''South American Overwatch Circuit: Season 2 - Division 1''' is the first division for the second season for the community South American Overwatch Circuit, featuring the best teams in the region.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': GSL Format
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top four teams''' advance to Playoffs.
**'''Four last placed teams''' are eliminated.
**'''Two last placed teams''' are demoted to [[South_American_Overwatch_Circuit/Season_3/Division_2|South American Overwatch Circuit: Season 3 - Division 2]]
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
