==Overview==
===Format===
* Group stage
** Four Groups with four teams in each
** Double elimination group format (GSL) {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Top two from each group move on to the playoffs

* Playoffs
** Single-Elimination Bracket
** Quarter/Semi finals will be {{Abbr/Bo3}} 
** Grand Finals will be {{Abbr/Bo5}}
