==About==
DreamHack Summer 2018 Overwatch is a BYOC LAN event held at DreamHack Summer 2018.

===Format===
* '''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
** Grand Finals and Winners' FInals match are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** All other matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
