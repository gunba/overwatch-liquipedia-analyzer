==About==
DreamHack Summer 2018 Overwatch Female Tournament is a BYOC LAN event held at DreamHack Summer 2018 for the Female community.

===Format===
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
