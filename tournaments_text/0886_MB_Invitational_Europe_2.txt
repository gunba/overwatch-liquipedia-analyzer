==Information==

===Description===
A tournament run by Monkey Bubble Ltd. which aims to take place every 3 calendar months. To get an invite to this tournament, teams must participate in all the tournaments Monkey Bubble Ltd. has recognised and displayed on their [https://monkeybubbleow.com/tournament/how-it-works/ tournament list] and perform well. <br>
The teams that perform the best and earn the most points from each tournament are invited to the Overwatch(R) MB European regional invitational. Note that for the first tournament (24th-26th May 2019), Monkey Bubble has elected to manually select teams, but for future tournaments they will be based on the MB Leaderboard system.


===Format===
* Double Elimination Tournament
** All matches Bo5 with tiebreaker
