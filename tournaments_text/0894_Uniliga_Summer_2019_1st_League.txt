==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 10 players.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Swiss System
**<span class="bg-up">Top eight (8) teams</span> advance to Playoffs.
**Each match will be played in a four-game-set.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**In case of a match draw a predetermined {{Abbr/Bo3}} control map will be played to decide the winner.
**Group Stage is divided into to phases.
***Each phase has it's own map pool.
***Swiss seeding is unaffected by a new phase, but rematches can happen.
**Teams are awarded 2 points per match win.
**If two teams are tied at the end of the group stage, tiebreakers are as follows:
***1st Mapscore, 2nd Head to head, 3rd Tiebraker match ({{Abbr/Bo1}} on [[Lijiang Tower]]).
*'''Playoffs''': Single-Elimination
**Each match (except finals) will be played in a First-to-3 series.
**Finals will be played in a First-to-4 series.
