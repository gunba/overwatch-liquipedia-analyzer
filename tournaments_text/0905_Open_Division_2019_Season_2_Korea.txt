==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Swiss Format
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Top sixteen (16) teams advance to the Playoffs of the [[Open_Division/2019/Season_2/Korea#Playoffs|Open Division 2019 Season 2 - Korea]].
** Bottom teams are eliminated.
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}, except Grand Final that is {{Abbr/Bo7}}
*** Winners' bracket advantage of 1 game.
