==Overview==

The '''Electronic Sports Festival''' is currently the biggest Esports-Event in Austria with a prizepool of 23.000 € spreading across all tournaments held at the LAN-Event.<br>
There are tournaments held in CS:GO, PUBG, League of Legends, Overwatch, Dota 2 and many more games.
