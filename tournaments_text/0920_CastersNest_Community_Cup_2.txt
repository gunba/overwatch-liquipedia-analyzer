==Overview==
'''CastersNest Community Cup''' is a series of tournaments organized by [https://discord.overtown.fr OverTown] and [http://twitch.tv/castersnest CastersNest].

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Swiss rounds
** All matches are '''Bo3'''.
** Top 16 advance to playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**Winners' Bracket: two first rounds in {{Abbr/Bo1}}, two last in {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Losers' Bracket: three first rounds in {{Abbr/Bo1}}, three last in {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand Finals: {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
