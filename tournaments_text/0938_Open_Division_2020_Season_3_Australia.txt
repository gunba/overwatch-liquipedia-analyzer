==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Swiss Format
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Top sixteen (16) teams advance to the Playoffs of the [[Open_Division/2020/Season_3/Australia#Playoffs|Open Division 2020 Season 3 - Australia]].
** Remaining teams are eliminated
* '''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
** Round of 16 and Quarter Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Semi Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** Grand Final is {{Abbr/Bo7}}
