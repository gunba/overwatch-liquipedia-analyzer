==Information==
===Format===
* '''Regular Bracket''': Double Elimination
** Round 1 and Round 2 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** All subsequent rounds are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** Bracket stops at {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 6}}, who all qualify for Final Bracket
* '''Final Bracket''': Double Elimination
** Top six teams from Regular Bracket and {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom <s>six</s> three teams}} from {{LeagueIconSmall/overwatch contenders trials}} [[Overwatch_Contenders/2020/Season_2/North_America/Trials/October|Trials October]]
** Round 1 and Round 2 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Winners' Semifinals and Losers' Round 3 to Losers' Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** Winners' Final and Grand Final are {{Abbr/Bo7}}
*** Grand Finalist from Winners Bracket starts Grand Finals winning 1-0
** {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} qualify for {{LeagueIconSmall/overwatch contenders trials}} [[Overwatch_Contenders/2020/Season_2/North_America/Trials/November|Trials November]]
