==About==

===Format===
*'''Group Stage:''' Round Robin
**Teams are split into six (6) groups of three (3) or four (4) teams each.
**Matches are played in a First to 2 format.
**The first map is chosen by the team on the left side of the match.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams from each group</span> advance to Playoffs.
**<span class="bg-stay">The remaining six (6) teams</span> face off in the Tie Breaker to qualify for Playoffs.
*'''Tie Breaker:''' Double Elimination
**Six (6) teams in a double elimination bracket.
**Matches are played in a First to 2 format.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**Top four (4) teams advance to Playoffs.
*'''Playoffs:''' Double Elimination
**16 teams in a double elimination bracket.
**Each match, except Grand Finals, will be played in a First to 3 format.
***Grand Finals are played in a First to 4 format.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**Top six (6) teams qualify for [[EBC/2020/EMEA/Major|EMEA Major]].
