==Information==
'''Huya Recalling Cup Overwatch Streamers Bounty Competition''' is overwatch tournament organized and mainly sponsored by [http://huya.com/ huya.com].

===Format===
*Team Events
** Quarter-finals and Semi-finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}. The 3rd place match is {{Abbr/Bo5}}. The final is {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
** Single Elimination bracket.
** The home teams select the first map; the guest temas select whether to attach or defend. In the succeeding rounds, the winner selects whether attach or defend, and the loser selects the next map,
