==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams</span> advance to Semifinals in Playoffs.
**<span class="bg-stayup">Places three (3) through six (6)</span> advance to Round of 6 in Playoffs.
**<span class="bg-staydown">Places eight (8) and nine (9)</span> are relegated to the Wildcard Tournament of the next season.
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom team</span> is relegated to 2nd League in the next season.
**Each match will be played in a four-game-set.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Top six (6) teams from Group Stage will be placed in the bracket according to their seeding.
***Top two (2) teams from Group Stage receive a first round bye.
**Round of 6 and Semifinals will be played in a First-to-3 series.
**Grand Finals will be played in a First-to-4 series.
