==Information==
===Description===
Rally for representation is an Overwatch tournament for the Female and Non-binary community, organised by [https://femalelegends.com/ Female Legends] and powered by [https://monkeybubble.gg/ Monkey Bubble]. 

===Format===
* Playoffs
** Double Elimination.
** Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}. All other matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
