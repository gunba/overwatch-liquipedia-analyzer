==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.

===Format===
*'''Group Stages''': Round Robin
**'''Group Stage 1''':
***Teams will be randomly seeded into five groups.
***<span class="bg-up">Top eight (8) teams</span> advance to '''Gold Groups''' in '''Group Stage 2'''.
***<span class="bg-stayup">Next best ten (10) teams</span> advance to '''Silver Groups''' in '''Group Stage 2'''.
***<span class="bg-staydown">Bottom ten (10) teams</span> advance to '''Bronze Groups''' in '''Group Stage 2'''.
**'''Group Stage 2'''
***Teams will be seeded into groups as follows:
****Two Gold Groups of four (4) teams each.
****Two Silver Groups of five (5) teams each.
****Two Bronze Groups of five (5) teams each.
***<span class="bg-up">Top (1) team from each '''Gold Group'''</span> advances to Quarterfinals in '''Playoffs'''.
***<span class="bg-stayup">Bottom three (3) teams from each '''Gold Group''', top two (2) from each '''Silver Group''' and top (1) team from each '''Bronze Group'''</span> advance to Round of 14 in '''Playoffs'''.
**Each match will be played in a four-game-set.
**The first map is predetermined per round.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**Teams are awarded 2 points per match win.
**If two teams are tied at the end of the group stage, tiebreakers are as follows:
***1st map difference, 2nd head to head, 3rd head to head on [[Control]].
*'''Playoffs''': Single-Elimination
**Top six (6) teams from Gold Groups and top two (2) teams from Silver Groups will be placed in the bracket according to their seeding.
***Top two (2) teams from Gold Groups will be seeded into Quarterfinals.
**Remaining teams in order of lowest to highest seed can choose their opponent in the Round of 14.
**Round of 14 and Quarterfinals will be played in a First-to-3 series.
**Semifinals and Finals will be played in a First-to-4 series.
