===Regional Qualifiers===
* Two regions: East and West
<section begin="Regionals"/>
* 16-team single-elimination tournament
* {{Abbr/Bo5}} matches
* Points awarded based on placement
* Each winner and top four teams per region by overall points advance to Finals
<section end="Regionals"/>

===Last Chance Qualifiers===
<section begin="LCQ"/>
* Inter-regional tournament
* 16-team single-elimination tournament
* {{Abbr/Bo5}} matches
* Top two teams advance to Finals
<section end="LCQ"/>

===Finals===
<section begin="Finals"/>
* 16-team group stage
* 8-team single-elimination bracket
All matches {{Abbr/Bo5}}
<section end="Finals"/>
