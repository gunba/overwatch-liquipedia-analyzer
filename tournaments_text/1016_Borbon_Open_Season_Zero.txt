==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Double Round-Robin
** Group Stage are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Playoff are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** 3 Groups Top (4) teams advance to the Playoffs of [[Borbon_Open_Season_Zero|Borbon Open Season Zero]].
** Bottom teams are eliminated.
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
* '''Player Elligibility'''
** Teams will have 4 contenders slots. these can be spread via the following:
***A player who finished Top 4 in any Contenders/ ACE season will be worth 2 slots.
***A player who hasn't finished top 4 but has competed in contenders will be worth 1 slot.
** Teams may only have 1 non OCE/PAC player.
