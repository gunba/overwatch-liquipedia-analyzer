The '''OPSE 2020-21 Season''' is a tournament organized by the Ontario Post-Secondary Esports Association.

==Information==
* One game per week for each team (12 games per team)
* Each regular season match is {{Abbr/Bo3}}
* Each playoff match is {{Abbr/Bo5}}
* Map format: Control/Assault or Hybrid/Escort and then Control until a winner is determined
