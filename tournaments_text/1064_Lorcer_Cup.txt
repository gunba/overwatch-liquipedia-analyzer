==Format==
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**3 qualifiers for CIS teams (For teams where at least one member is fluent in Russian)
**1 open qualifier
*'''Group stage:'''
**2 groups of 4 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}.
**Dual Tournament Format.
**Top 2 advance to the Playoffs.
*'''Playoffs:'''
**Single-elimination bracket.
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}.
**Finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}.
