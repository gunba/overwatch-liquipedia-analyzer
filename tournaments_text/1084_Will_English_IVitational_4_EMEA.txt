'''Will English IVitational 4 - EMEA''' is a tournament organized by Will English IV.

==Information==
*Group Stage
**Round Robin
***2 Groups (Group A: 4 Teams, Group B: 5 Teams)
***First to 2

*Playoffs
**8-Team Double elimination - First to 2
***Winner's, Loser's & Grand Finals - First to 3
