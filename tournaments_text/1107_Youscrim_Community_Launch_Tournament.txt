===Format===
*Single elimination
**Round of 32 are "First to 2"
**Round of 16 are "First to 2"
**Quarterfinals are "First to 3"
**Semifinals are "First to 3"
**Finals are "First to 4"
**Match for 3rd place is "First to 3"
