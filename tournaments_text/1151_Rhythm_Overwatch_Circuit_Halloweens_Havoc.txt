==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage:'''
**Group A:
***<span class="bg-seedup">Top 3</span> teams from last tournaments play for seeding in playoffs
***All matches are {{abbr/Bo5}}
**Groups B and C:
***<span class="bg-seedup">Top1-2</span> advance to winners bracket, <span class="bg-up">Top3-4</span> advance to losers bracket
***All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}
*'''Playoffs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 11 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand Finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}
