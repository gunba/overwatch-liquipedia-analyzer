'''Nittany Invitational 2023''' is a tournament organized by Matthew "[[Plasmashark4]]" Combs and Christian "[[DMT]]" Wasta.

==Information==

===Format===
* Round-robin group stage to determine which two teams enter the playoffs
** Matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
* Top two teams play a {{Abbr/Bo5}} to determine winner
