==Information==
*Japan and HK&TW delegation are selected by their Community Lead of World Cup.
*Chinese player eligibility (satisfying one of the following):
**Former female overwatch tournaments enrollment. No ill conduct.
**Former blizzard's overwatch tournaments enrollment.
**Sponsored by at least 2 players in different teams or 2 practitioners (manager, pro-player, coach, leader, commentator, referee, former blizzard employer, etc.)

===Format===
*'''Group stage:''' ''July 6<sup>th</sup> - July 21<sup>st</sup>''
**Round Robin with 2 Groups
** {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} in each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoff:''' ''July 21<sup>th</sup> - August 4<sup>th</sup>''
** Double Elimination bracket
