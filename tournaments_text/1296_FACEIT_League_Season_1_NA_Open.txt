==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''April 29<sup>th</sup> - June 9<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**14 Swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 32 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 10<sup>th</sup> - June 25<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Double elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}, all other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}

===Promotion===
*{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 16 teams}} are promoted to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/NA/Expert|Season 2 Expert]]
*{{Bgcolortext|up|17th-24th teams}} are promoted to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/NA/Advanced|Season 2 Advanced]]
