==Information==
'''PolyLAN 39''' is a LAN event in Switzerland, offering tournaments in various esport titles. It is organised by students of the EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne).

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': ''May 10<sup>th</sup>''
**6 Swiss rounds
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4}} teams advance to '''Playoffs'''
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
*'''Playoffs:''' ''May 10<sup>th</sup> - 11<sup>th</sup>''
**Double elimination bracket
**All matches ''(excl. Grand Finals)'' are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
