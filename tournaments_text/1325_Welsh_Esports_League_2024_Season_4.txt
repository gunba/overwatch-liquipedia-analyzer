==Information==
*The Welsh Esports League, created by [https://esportswales.org Esports Wales], using the [https://hub.esportswales.org Esports Wales Hub], is an Esports League designed to highlight the potential of Welsh Esports.
**Each team must contain a minimum of two Welsh players, including substitutes.

===Format===
*Group Stage
**Round Robin with 2 Groups
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
*Playoffs
**Single Elimination bracket
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
