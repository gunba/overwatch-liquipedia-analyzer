==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #20''' is the 20th European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/18|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #18]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/19|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #19]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/21|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #21]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/22|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #22]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/October_2016|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals -October]].
