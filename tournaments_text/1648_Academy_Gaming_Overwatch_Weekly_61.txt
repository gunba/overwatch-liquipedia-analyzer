==Overview==
The '''Academy Gaming Overwatch Weekly #61''' is the 61st weekly tournament hosted by ''Academy Gaming''.

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
**All rounds before the quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo1}}; quarterfinals, semifinals, and final are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Round of 32: [[Hollywood]]
**Round of 16: [[Route 66]]
**Round of 8: [[Dorado]] -> [[King's Row]] -> [[Nepal]]
**Semifinals and Finals use [http://owdraft.com/ OWDraft] for map drafting on stream.
