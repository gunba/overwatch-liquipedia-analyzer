==About==
[[Le Hive Esport]] will run its first BYOC LAN event on Saturday, 13 May 2017: the [[Cannes Overwatch Cup]]. 8 teams of 6 players will fight at the [[MJC Picaud]] in Cannes.

===Cancelled===

On May 9th, 2017, '''Le Hive eSport''' announces that the Cannes Overwatch Cup is cancelled due to the lack of participants. <ref name="COWC cancelled">[https://www.facebook.com/LeHiveeSport/posts/1954962894736353:0 Cannes Overwatch Cup is cancelled]</ref>

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': {{Abbr/Bo3}} format.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
