==About==
===Format===
*First Group Stage
**4 groups of 4 teams.
**Round Robin.
**{{Abbr/Bo3}} matches.
**Last team from each group is eliminated.
*Second Group Stage
**4 groups of 3 teams.
**Round Robin.
**{{Abbr/Bo3}} matches.
**Last team from each group is eliminated.
*Playoffs
**Double Elimination Bracket.
**{{Abbr/Bo3}} matches, winner and loser bracket final in {{Abbr/Bo5}} and Grand Final in {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
