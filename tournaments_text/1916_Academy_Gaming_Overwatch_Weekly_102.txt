==Overview==
The '''Academy Gaming Overwatch Weekly #102''' is the 102nd weekly tournament hosted by [[Academy Gaming]].

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
**All rounds before the quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo1}}; quarterfinals, semifinals, and final are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Sudden death map of the week: [[Oasis]]
**Round of 32: [[Lijiang Tower]]
**Round of 16: [[Eichenwalde]]
**Round of 8: [[Ilios]] -> [[Route 66]] -> [[Numbani]]
**Semifinals and Finals use OWDraft for map drafting on stream.
