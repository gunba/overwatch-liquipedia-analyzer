==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #88''' is the 88th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/89|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #89]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/90|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #90]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/91|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #91]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/February_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - February]].
