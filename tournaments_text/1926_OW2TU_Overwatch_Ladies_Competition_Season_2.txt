==Information==
===Format===
* Group stage
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** 2 points awarded for each map victory, -1 point for each map loss.
** The 4 teams that earn the most points proceed to Playoffs.
* Playoffs
** Single Elimination
