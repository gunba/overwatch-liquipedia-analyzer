==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #85''' is the 85th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/84|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #84]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/86|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #86]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/87|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #87]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/January_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - January]].
