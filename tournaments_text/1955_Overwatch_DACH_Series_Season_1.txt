==About==

The '''Overwatch DACH Series''' is the official league of DACH eSports. Eight Teams representing regions from Germany, Austria and Switzerland will compete against each other.
==Format==

*'''Group Stage''':
**Each team will play every other team once in a '''Round Robin''' style league.
**Every match will consist of a '''4-map set'''. The first map will be decided by the organizers before the match, every following map is the choice of the previous map's loser.
**Standings tiebreakers:
***Number of match wins
***Cumulative map differential
*'''Playoffs''':
**The top four teams of the group stage will qualify for the '''offline finals'''.
**The offline finals will take place during the DACH community meetup in '''Aschaffenburg, Germany'''.
**Playoffs will consist of a '''Single-Elimination Bracket'''.
**The Semifinals and the 3rd-place match will be '''first to 3''', the Grand Finale will be '''first to 4'''.
