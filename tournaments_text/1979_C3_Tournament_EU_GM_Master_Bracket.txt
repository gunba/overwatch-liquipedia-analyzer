==Information==
===Description===
C3 Tournament is an Overwatch charity tournament for the Female and Non-binary community, created by The Cat Collective, Team Contemno, Galactic Guardians, Ganymede's Girls, Helix, Hunters of Artemis, Team Paradox, and Valkyrja. 

===Prize Pool===
The funds raised for this tournament were donated directly to Ipas organization to support their activities to advance women's reproductive health and rights and to prevent abortion-related deaths and injuries.<br>The event raised $1958 {{Abbr/USD}}.
