==Information==

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
*Quarter-Final and Semi-Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
*The Final will be {{Abbr/Bo5}}
*A 2-2-2 role lock is implemented in this tournament.

===Qualification===
*'''Top 6''' teams in average rankings of all the 10 applicants directly enter in the Main Event. 
*The other 4 teams go into the Qualifier and the two winners advance to the Main Event.
