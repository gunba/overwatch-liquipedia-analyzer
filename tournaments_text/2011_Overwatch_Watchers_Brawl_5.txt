==Information==
The '''Overwatch Watcher's Brawl V''' is the fifth tournament of the series played in May 2019, hosted by [https://twitter.com/TheWatchersOW The Watchers]. Matches ran from May 6th - 31st, 2019. All matches are casted and uploaded to [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UfvCT1izNQU&list=PLCL2_wiOe4CFKGSinubFABupbwnOhiwpr The Watcher's Youtube] channel. There was a $30 USD per team entry fee to this tournament.

===Format===
* Double Elimination
* Matches are Best of Five ({{Abbr/Bo5}})
