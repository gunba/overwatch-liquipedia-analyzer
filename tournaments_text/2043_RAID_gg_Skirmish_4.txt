==Information==
The '''RAID.gg Skirmish #4''' is the fourth tournament of the series played in Febuary 2019<ref>[https://battlefy.com/raid/raid-skirmish-2019-overwatch-round-1/5c518072acacd9031f76c3ea/info?infoTab=details RAID Skirmish 2019 - Overwatch Round 1]</ref>, hosted by [[RAID Skirmish|RAID Gaming]]. 
Participating teams must be made up of players within the following countries: all countries within the European Economic Area (the European Union, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein) and Switzerland.
