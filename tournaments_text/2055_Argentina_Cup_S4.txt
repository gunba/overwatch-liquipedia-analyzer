==Information==

===Format===

*'''Placement Stage'''
**Single round robin.
**Each match is a best of 3.
**The teams are divided in four divisions according its final position in the table.
*'''Group Stage'''
**Single round robin.
**Each match is a best of 3.
**The top 8 teams of the Group Stage qualify for Playoffs.
*'''Playoffs'''
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
