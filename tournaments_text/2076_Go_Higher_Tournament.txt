==Information==
===Format===
*10 teams in a '''Group Round''' format.
*'''Group Round''' will be played in a '''Best of 3''' format. 
**First and Second maps will be [[Assault]], [[Hybrid]] or [[Escort]]. The third map will obligatorily be a [[Control]] map, chosen by the team that loses the map 2.
**For map selection, in the first game team A must choose the type of map ([[Escort]], [[Assault]] or [[Hybrid]]) and team B must choose a map that fits this category; in the second game, team B will choose the type of map (excluding the type already chosen previously) and team A will choose a map from among the type options.
*'''Elimination Round''' will be played in a '''Best of 5''' format.
**The first control map will be chosen at random. In the sequence, the team that loses chooses the next map, among the options of the map type.
** Map Order: [[Control]] → [[Hybrid]] → [[Assault]] → [[Escort]] → [[Control]].
