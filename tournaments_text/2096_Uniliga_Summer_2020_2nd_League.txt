==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.

===Format===
*Swiss System with twelve (12) rounds.
**<span class="bg-up">Top team</span> is promoted to 1st League in the next season.
**<span class="bg-stayup">Places two (2) and three (3)</span> are invited to the Wildcard Tournament of the next season.
**Each match will be played in a four-game-set.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**Teams are seeded by (1) wins, (2) map difference, (3) head to head wins, (4) head to head map difference and (5) most points obtained by opponent, except best and worst.
