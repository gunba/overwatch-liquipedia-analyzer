==About==

The '''ScuffedBux''' is a series of tournaments organized by the [https://battlefy.com/scuffed-cast/ Scuffed Cast] organization. This specific edition is called '''MOBA Charity Edition''' as it is organized to raise funds for the Italian red cross in reaction to the COVID-19 2020 european outbreak. In total, over 1,700€ have been raised over the week-end the tournament occured on.
