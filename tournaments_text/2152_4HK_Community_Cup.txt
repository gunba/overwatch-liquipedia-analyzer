==Information==
===Player Elligibility===
* Each team is required to have 6 players residing in Taiwan, Hong Kong or Macau
** Teams are allowed to have substitutes not from the above-mentioned countries, however at any point in the match, only one foreign player is allowed to be fielded
===Format===
* Seeding
** Teams are randomly seeded, since there are 17 teams, two teams will be required to play a knockout match to determine the 16th team in the bracket.
* Single Elimination Bracket
** Round of 16 and Quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Semi-Finals and Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
