'''ICA Overwatch''' is a Spanish tournament organized by ICA Series.

==Information==
*<!--Rules, including map rotation, "best of" number of maps, and tournament format-->
===Format===
* '''Group Stage'''
** All matches consist of 3 maps.
** Top 2 teams of each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
* '''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
** Semifinal matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Final matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
