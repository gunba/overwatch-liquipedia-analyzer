The '''Pacific Prequel Invitational''' is a tournament organized by [https://twitter.com/ggwplikeus Gamers Like Us] curated to showcase the potential of the Pacific region in Overwatch 2. It is part of the Pacific Prequel Series.

===Format===
*Double Elimination.
*The finals will be {{Abbr/Bo5}}. The other matches will be {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
