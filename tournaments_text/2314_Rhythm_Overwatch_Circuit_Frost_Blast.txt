==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage:'''
**Group A:
***<span class="bg-seedup">Top 4</span> teams from last tournaments play for seeding in playoffs
***All matches are {{abbr/Bo5}}
**Groups B, C, D and E:
***<span class="bg-seedup">Top 1</span> advance to winners bracket, <span class="bg-up">Top 2</span> advance to losers bracket
***All matches are {{abbr/Bo5}}
*'''Playoffs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 12 teams
**Winners bracket matches are {{abbr/Bo5}}, Losers bracket matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand Finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}
