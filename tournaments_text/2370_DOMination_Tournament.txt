'''DOMination Tournament''' is a tournament organized by DOM, Discord server.

==Information==
===Format===
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**Round Robin with 2 groups, each 6 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}
**'''Top 4 of each group''' advance to the play-offs.
*'''Play-offs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 8 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand-finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}
