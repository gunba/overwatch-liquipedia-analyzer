'''''Notes: All the player must be under 20 years old.'''''

===Format===
* '''Group Stage'''
** Round Robin in each group. (3 Groups)
** Matches in '''Group stage''' are {{Abbr/Ft2}}
** <span class="bg-up">Top two (6)</span> in each group advance to the '''Playoffs Qualifier'''.
* '''Playoffs Qualifier'''
** Single Elimination Bracket. Each match is {{Abbr/Ft3}}.
** Winners in '''Playoffs Qualifier''' advance to the '''Playoffs'''.
** Three teams losers  go to the '''Last Chance Qualifier'''.
*'''Last Chance Qualifier'''
** Round Robin. (1 Group)
** <span class="bg-up">Top one (1)</span> advance to the '''Playoffs'''.
** Each match is {{Abbr/Bo1}}.
** Map is '''Lijiang Tower'''.
* '''Playoffs'''
** Single Elimination Bracket. Each match is {{Abbr/Ft3}}. Grand Final is {{Abbr/Ft4}}
