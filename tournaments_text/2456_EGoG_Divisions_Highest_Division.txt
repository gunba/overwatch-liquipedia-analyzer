==Information==
===Format===
*Only CIS based teams are allowed. Any team is allowed to have up to 2 players from non-CIS region.
*'''Group Stage:''' ''April 8<sup>th</sup> - April 30<sup>th</sup>''
**Swiss format
**2 Groups
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2}} teams of each group advance to '''Playoffs'''
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}} 
**'''Top 2 teams''' from each group advance to the play-offs.
*'''Playoffs:''' ''May 5<sup>th</sup> - June 2<sup>nd</sup>''
**Double elimination bracket
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
