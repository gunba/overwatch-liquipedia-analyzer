==Format==
'''Division Structure''':
*The league is divided into four (4) divisions with six (6) teams.

'''Group Stage:'''
*Round Robin over five (5) Game Days
**All matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
*After five (5) game days, a Swiss System is implemented

'''Swiss System:'''
*<span class="bg-up">The teams in 1st and 2nd place</span> from each division enter the first Swiss System.
*<span class="bg-stayup">The teams in 3rd and 4th place</span> in each division enter the second Swiss System.
*<span class="bg-stay">Teams in 5th and 6th place</span> in each division enter the third Swiss System

'''Promotion Opportunities:'''
*The top four (4) teams from Swiss System 1 compete in the Upper Relegation Battle against teams from the 2nd League.
*Teams placed 5th to 7th in Swiss System 1 enter the Lower Bracket Relegation.
*The 8th place team from Swiss System 1 and the 1st place team from Swiss System 2 play a Decider Match for the final spot in the Relegation Bracket, which is also in the Lower Bracket.
