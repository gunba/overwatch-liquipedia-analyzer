==Information==
The '''Overwatch Watcher's Brawl I''' is the first tournament of the series played in January 2019 hosted by [https://twitter.com/TheWatchersOW The Watchers]. Matches ran from January 6th - 31st, 2019. All matches are casted and uploaded to [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLCL2_wiOe4CHXu2Zyw_CZnXj51xALswco The Watcher's Youtube] channel.

===Format===
* Double Elimination
* Matches are Best of Three ({{Abbr/Bo3}})
* Finals and last loser's bracket are Best of Five ({{Abbr/Bo5}})
