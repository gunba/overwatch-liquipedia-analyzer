==Information==

The '''RAID.gg Skirmish #3''' is the third tournament of a monthly series played in October 2018<ref>[https://battlefy.com/raid/raid-skirmish-week-10-overwatch/5ba3be99164e6c05844d5e21/info?infoTab=details RAID Skirmish Week 10]</ref>, hosted by [[RAID.gg|RAID Gaming]]. Participating teams are required to have 4/6 of their members to be Residents of Ireland or Northern Ireland.
