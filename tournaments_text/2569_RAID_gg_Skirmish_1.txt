==Information==
The '''RAID.gg Skirmish #1''' is the kick off tournament of a monthly series starting in August 2018<ref>[https://battlefy.com/raid/raid-skirmish-week-1-overwatch/5b58ea643a8fbf03d477d1dc/info?infoTab=details RAID Skirmish Week 1]</ref>, hosted by [[RAID.gg|RAID Gaming]]. 
Participating teams are required to have 4/6 of their members to be Residents of Ireland or Northern Ireland.
