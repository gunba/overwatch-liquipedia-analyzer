==Information==
'''SnasCUP''' is a [[:Category:Europe|Spanish]] tournament for amateur teams.

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode.
* '''Playoffs''' -
**Double elimination.
** Winners' Bracket
*** Round 1 and Quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo1}}.
*** Semifinals and Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Losers' Bracket
*** Round 1, 2 and 3 are {{Abbr/Bo1}}.
*** Round 4, Semifinals and Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
*** Winner from winner's bracket takes advantage of 1-0 in Grand Finals.
