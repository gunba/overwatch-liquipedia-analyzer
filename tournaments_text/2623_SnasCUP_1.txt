==Information==
'''SnasCUP''' is a [[:Category:Europe|Spanish]] tournament for amateur teams.

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
* '''Playoffs''': Double elimination
** Winners' Finals, Losers' Round 4, Losers' Semifinals and Losers' Finals are bo3
**Grand Finals are bo5
