==About==
===Format===
*4 teams from [[Overwatch APEX/Season 1|Overwatch APEX Season 1]].
*2 teams from [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 1|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 1]].
*2 teams from [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 2|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 2]].
*'''Group Stage''': GSL format.
**The top 2 challenger teams will choose their opponents, in descending order based on their APEX Points.
**The remaining teams are allocated into groups randomly. 
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group qualify for [[Overwatch APEX/Season 2|Overwatch APEX Season 2]].
