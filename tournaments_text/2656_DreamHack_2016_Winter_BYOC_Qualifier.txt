==About==

The BYOC qualifier will be hosted live onsite at DreamHack Winter 2016, with the top four teams advancing to the main event.

===Format===
*Top 4 of the BYOC qualifier directly qualifies for the Main Event.
*Double-Elimination Bracket in ({{Abbr/Bo3}}).
*Qualifying rounds in ({{Abbr/Bo5}}).
