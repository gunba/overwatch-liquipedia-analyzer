==About==
The '''Overwatch APEX Season 1: Offline Qualifier #2''' will feature 56 teams from [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_1/Online_Qualifier/2|Online Qualifier #2]] and 6 teams from [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_1|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 1]]. The top 8 teams will qualify for [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 1|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 2]]

===Format===
*Offline Qualifiers: November 5, 2016 to November 6, 2016.
