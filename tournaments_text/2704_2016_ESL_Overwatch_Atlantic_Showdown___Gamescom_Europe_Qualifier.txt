'''Europe Main Qualifier''' will allow 4 teams to advance to the [[2016 ESL Overwatch Atlantic Showdown - Gamescom|main event]] at Gamescom.

==Information==
===Format===
*Game mode: Best of Five
*Maps: The first map will be preselected. 
**The losing team of each map will be able to pick the next map.
***This weeks map pool: [[Dorado]], [[Hollywood]], [[Hanamura]], [[Ilios]], [[King's Row]], [[Temple of Anubis]]
*Hero selection limit: 1 per team
**All heroes are currently allowed
*Disable kill cam: On
*High bandwidth: On
*Rule set: Competitive
