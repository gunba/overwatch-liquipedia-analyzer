===Format===
*'''General''':
**Matches:
***Single Elimination
***All matches are Bo3.
**Maps:
***First map will be random.
***The losing team of each map will be able to pick the next map.
***If the series is tied a control map will be picked randomly, 2 teams will compete for the first spot.
**Heroes:
***Less than two same heroes will be allowed (including two).
