==About==
The '''Overwatch APEX Season 4: Offline Qualifier''' will feature 14 teams from [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_4/Online_Qualifier|Online Qualifier]] and two (2) teams from [[Open_Division/2017/Season_1/Korea|Open Division - KR S1]]. The top 7 teams will qualify for [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 5|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 5]]

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*'''Group Stage #1''': Round Robin
**'''Top 2 teams''' advance to '''Group Stage #2'''.
*'''Group Stage #2''': Round Robin
**'''Top 3''' teams for each group qualify for [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_5|APEX Challengers Season 5]]
*'''Wildcard''': Single Elimination
**'''Winner qualify''' for [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_5|APEX Challengers Season 5]]
