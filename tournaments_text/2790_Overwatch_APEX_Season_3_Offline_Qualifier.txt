==About==
The '''Overwatch APEX Season 3: Offline Qualifier''' will feature 14 teams from [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_3/Online_Qualifier|Online Qualifier]], one (1) team from [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_3|APEX CS S3]] and one (1) team from [[Road_to_APEX|Road to APEX]]. The top 3 teams will qualify for [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 4|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 4]]

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**'''Top 2 teams''' advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**'''Winners qualify''' for [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_4|APEX Challengers Season 4]]
*'''Wildcard''': Single Elimination
**'''Winner qualify''' for [[Overwatch_APEX/Challengers/Season_4|APEX Challengers Season 4]]
