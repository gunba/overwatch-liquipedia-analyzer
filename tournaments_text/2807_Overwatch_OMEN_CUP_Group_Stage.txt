==About==
===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competition mode.
*'''Group Stage #1''': Round Robin
** 64 teams are divided into 4 ''blocks''. Each ''block'', up to 4 teams in 1 group, divided into a maximum of 4 groups.
** '''Top 2''' of each group advance to '''Group Stage #2 (playoffs)'''.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo3}} round will be played on [[Lijiang Tower]] map.
*'''Group Stage #2''': Double Elimination
**'''Top 2''' of each ''block'' (8 teams) advance to '''Playoffs'''.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo3}} round will be played on [[Lijiang Tower]] map.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination.
**Semifinals & Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo3}} round will be played on [[Ilios]] map.
