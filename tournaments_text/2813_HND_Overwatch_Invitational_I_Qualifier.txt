==Overview==
* '''Qualifiers''' will be run in a round robin format on April 1st. Each team in a group must play one another in a BO1 format, the top two teams with the most wins in their group will move to the Finals Bracket.
* '''Finals''' will be a double elimination bracket held on April 2nd. There will be 8 total spots, filled by the top two teams of each Round Robin group. All games will be BO3, Grand Finals will be BO5.
* Top four finishing teams will be automatically invited to the [https://liquipedia.net/overwatch/HND_Overwatch_Invitational/1| HND: Overwatch Invitational #1]
