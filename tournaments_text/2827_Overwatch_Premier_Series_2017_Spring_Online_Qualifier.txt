==Information==

===Format===
*'''Stage #1''': Single Elimination
**4 groups for 48 teams.
**Top 3 of each group advance to [[Overwatch Premier Series/2017/Spring/Preseason|Preseason]].
**4 - 6th teams of each group advance to '''Stage #2'''.
*'''Stage #2''': Round Robin
**Winner of the qualification bracket advance to [[Overwatch Premier Series/2017/Spring/Preseason|Preseason]].
