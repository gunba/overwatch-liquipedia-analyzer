==About==
===Format===
The teams with the top 6 circuit ratings who did not qualify directly through the qualification circuit will be invited to play in the wildcards. These wildcard matches will qualify the final 3 ANZ teams into the main event, for a total of 16 ANZ teams, consisting of 12 qualified teams plus the 4 invited teams.
