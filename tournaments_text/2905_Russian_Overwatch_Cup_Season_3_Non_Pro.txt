==About==
In Russian Overwatch Cup Pro division can play any team.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**'''Four''' groups of '''six''' teams each.
**All matches are '''{{Abbr/Bo3}}'''.
**'''Top 3 of each group advance to playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''':
**<span class="bg-up">Top one (1) teams</span> advance to the ''Quarterfinals'';
**<span class="bg-stayup">Top two (2) and three (3) teams</span> advance to the ''Round of 12''.
**''Round of 12'', ''Quarterfinals'' and ''Semifinal'' are '''{{Abbr/Bo3}}''', while ''Grand Final'' is '''{{Abbr/Bo5}}'''.

====Map Picks====
*The order of map picks is set according to rounds: '''first map''' is always a '''control map''' and set by administration. The way teams '''pick next maps''' is described below:
**'''Team that won''' a map '''bans one of the five maps''' according to round rules;
**'''Team that lost''' a map '''picks next map''' from the rest of four maps;
**'''Winning''' team picks side.
