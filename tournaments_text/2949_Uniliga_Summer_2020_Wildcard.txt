==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.
*Winners will qualify for the 1st League of the Uniliga.
*Losers will play in the 2nd League of the Uniliga.

===Format===
*Single Elimination
*Qualifying Slots depend on accepted invites for the 1st League.
*Teams are seeded according to their Skill Rating.
*Matches are played in a First-to-3 format.
