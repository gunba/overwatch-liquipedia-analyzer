The '''UGC Collegiate Fall Season Regular Season''' is a tournament organized by Ultimate Gaming Championship.

==Information==
* All teams will be randomly divided into two divisions
* 8 teams in each division
* Each team will play other teams in their division twice throughout the season
* Best-of-Five (BO5) game series
* One broadcasted show match per week
