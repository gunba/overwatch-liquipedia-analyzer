==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.
*Winners will qualify for the 1st League of the Uniliga.
*Losers will play in the 2nd League of the Uniliga.

===Format===<!--
*Double Elimination
**Top two (2) seeds start in the upper bracket finals.
**Bottom three (3) seeds start in the lower bracket.
*Qualifying Slots depend on accepted invites for the 1st League.
*Matches are played in a First-to-3 format.
*All maps in active in competitive play are allowed.
*First map is decided by tournament administration.
*All other maps are picked by the loser previous map.
*Map types are played in this order: 1. Control, 2. Escort, 3. Hybrid, 4. Assault.
