==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.

===Format===
*Single Elimination
**Bottom four (4) teams from the [[Uniliga/Winter 2020/1st League|1st League]] will be placed in the bracket as seeds #1-#4.
**Top eight (8) teams from the [[Uniliga/Winter 2020/2nd League|2nd League]] wil be placed in the bracket as seeds #5-#12.
**Seeds #1-#8 start in the Upper Bracket, seeds #9-#12 start in the Lower Bracket.
**All matches will be played in a First-to-3 series.
**Top four (4) teams will qualify for the 1st League in the next season.
