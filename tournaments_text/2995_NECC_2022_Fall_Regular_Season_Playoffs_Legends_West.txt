==Information==
===Format===
*Competitive Play ruleset
*Open only to paying NECC Members
*Legends division is comprised of top collegiate teams which is determined by NECC Activation games.

*Regular Season:
**Teams split into regional conferences
**Nine week round robin
**The top 8 teams are invited to playoffs where they can receive more points for nationals
**Nationals points are be awarded as follows (teams awarded the most points will be invited to attend nationals):
***Regular season finish
****75 - 1st Place (Regular Season Champions)
****60 - 2nd Place
****55 - 3rd Place
****50 - 4th Place
****45 - 5th Place
****40 - 6th Place
****35 - 7th Place
****30 - 8th Place
****25 - 9th Place
****20 - 10th Place
***Playoff finish
****40 - Quarterfinal Appearance & Loss
****60 - Semifinal Appearance & Loss
****80 - Championship Appearance & Loss
****100 - Championship Victory
***Totals
****175 - Maximum amount of points awarded per semester of competition
****20 - Minimum amount of points awarded per semester of competition
***Not all teams who qualify for nationals will attend, meaning there could be a fairly large gap in placements
*Nationals:
**A double elimination group bracket stage where the top 2 teams of each group go to the Bracket stage
**A single elimination bracket stage where the winner takes all
