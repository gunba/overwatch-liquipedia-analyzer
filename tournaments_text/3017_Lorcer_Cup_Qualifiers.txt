==Format==
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**3 qualifiers for CIS teams (For teams where at least one member is fluent in Russian)
**1 open qualifier
**Single-elimination bracket.
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top 2 of each qualifier''' advance to the [[Lorcer_Cup|Main Event]].
