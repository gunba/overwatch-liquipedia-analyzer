==Information==
===Format===
*Competitive Play ruleset
*Open only to paying NECC Members

* '''Regular Season:''' ''September 21st - November 9th, 2023''
** Teams split into two groups
** Seven week round robin
** The top 2 teams per group qualify for Playoffs
* '''Playoffs:''' ''November 15th - December 7th, 2023''
** Double-elimination bracket
*** All matches prior to Grand Final is {{Abbr|Ft3|First to 3}}
*** Grand Final is {{Abbr|Ft4|First to 4}}
