==Information==
*'''Qualifier''':
**'''Group Stage''': ''November 18<sup>th</sup> - 19<sup>th</sup>, 2023''
***Groups of 8 teams
***Round robin matches
***{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} from each group advance to Bracket Stage
**'''Bracket Stage''': ''November 18<sup>th</sup> - 19<sup>th</sup>, 2023''
***Eight single-elimination brackets
***{{Bgcolortext|up|Winner}} of each bracket advances to Knockouts
*'''Knockouts''': ''November 22<sup>nd</sup> - 25<sup>th</sup>, 2023''
**Double elimination bracket
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
***{{Bgcolortext|up|Winner}} advances to [[Flash Ops/Holiday Showdown/APAC|APAC Finals]]
