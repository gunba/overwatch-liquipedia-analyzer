===Format===
*'''Group Stage''' - ''October 23rd - 24th, 2023''
**67 teams - split into 11 groups of 4 and 3 groups of 5.
***Round Robin - all matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
****<span class="bg-up">Winner</span> of each group qualifies to the Round 2 of the [[#Bracket Stage|Bracket Stage]].
****<span class="bg-stay">Places 2 and 3</span> of each group qualify to Round 1 of the [[#Bracket Stage|Bracket Stage]].
****<span class="bg-down">4th/5th place</span> of each group is eliminated.
*'''Bracket Stage''' - ''October 25th - 26th, 2023''
**Single Elimination Bracket.
***<span class="bg-up">Top 2</span> teams from each group move on to [[North_Carolina_Varsity_Esports_and_STEM_League/Season_2/Regional|Regional Finals]]
****All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
