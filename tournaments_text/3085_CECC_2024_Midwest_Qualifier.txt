==Information==
*Open to colleges from Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, North Dakota, Ohio, South Dakota, Wisconsin, Manitoba, Nunavut, and Saskatchewan

**Single-elimination seeding stage for Midwest Regional
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
