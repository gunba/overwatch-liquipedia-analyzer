==About==
{{box|start|padding=2em}}
* '''West:''' September ''9<sup>th</sup>'' - ''17<sup>th</sup>''
**<span class="bg-stayup">4th-10th place teams</span> from [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Summer Stage/Qualifiers|Summer Stage Qualifiers]]
**'''Double Elimination Bracket'''
***9th and 10th-place teams play one knockout match
***Bracket stops at Upper and Lower Finals
***All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
***<span class="bg-up">Top 2 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Playoffs|Playoffs]]
{{box|break|padding=2em}}
* '''East:''' September ''15<sup>th</sup>'' - ''17<sup>th</sup>''
**All Overwatch League teams who do not directly qualify for playoffs.
**'''Double Elimination Bracket'''
***All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
