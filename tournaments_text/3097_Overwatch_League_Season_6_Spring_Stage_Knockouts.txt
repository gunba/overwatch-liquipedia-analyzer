==About==
{{box|start|padding=2em}}
* '''East:''' May ''20<sup>th</sup>'' - June ''4<sup>th</sup>''
**<span class="bg-up">Top 6 teams</span> from [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Opens|Spring Stage Opens]] and <span class="bg-up">all 6 teams</span> from [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Qualifiers|Spring Stage Qualifiers]]
**Two 6-team double-elimination brackets
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**<span class="bg-up">Top team</span> from each bracket advances to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Midseason Madness|Midseason Madness]]
{{box|break|padding=2em}}
* '''West:''' June ''2<sup>nd</sup>'' - June ''4<sup>th</sup>''
**<span class="bg-stayup">3rd-10th place teams</span> from [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Qualifiers|Spring Stage Qualifiers]]
**8-team double-elimination bracket
**Top 4 teams start in the upper bracket, bottom 4 teams start in the lower bracket
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**<span class="bg-up">Top 2 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Midseason Madness|Midseason Madness]]
{{box|end}}
