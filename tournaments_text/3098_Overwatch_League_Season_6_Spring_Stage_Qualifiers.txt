==About==
*Prior to the Qualifiers, the league announced that the '''{{Team|cdh}}''' have gone inactive as they  "continue to contemplate the future direction of the team." <ref name="hiatus">{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/overwatchleague/status/1648386565851299840|title=Ahead of our season start, we wanted to share with the community that the Chengdu Hunters are not included in today's schedule announcement.|publisher='''[[Overwatch League]]'''|date=2023-04-18}}</ref>
{{box|start|padding=2em}}
* '''West:''' April ''27<sup>th</sup>'' - May ''28<sup>th</sup>''
**Each team plays 8 matches
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**<span class="bg-seedup">Top 2 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Midseason Madness|Midseason Madness]]
**<span class="bg-up">3rd-6th place teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Knockouts|Spring Stage Knockouts]] upper bracket
**<span class="bg-stayup">7th-10th place teams</span> advance to Spring Stage Knockouts lower bracket
{{box|break|padding=2em}}
* '''East:''' April ''29<sup>th</sup>'' - May ''14<sup>th</sup>''
**Single Round Robin
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Matches between the three Korea-based teams ([[Dallas Fuel]], [[Seoul Dynasty]], [[Seoul Infernal]]) are played offline
**<span class="bg-stayup">All 6 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Knockouts|Spring Stage Knockouts]]
***<span class="bg-up">Top 4 teams</span> earn a first-round bye
{{box|end}}
*{{Hero icon/Lifeweaver}} [[Lifeweaver]] is available for play.
