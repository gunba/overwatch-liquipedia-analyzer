===Format===
*Swiss bracket
**Thirty (30) teams face off in a swiss bracket.
**Top 16 teams move on to LAN
*First map is pre-determined, following maps are picked by the loser of the previous map.
**The first map is Control.
**The second map is Escort or Hybrid.
**The third map is Push.
***This pattern repeats if needed.
*All matches are best of 3.
*Players must be enrolled in an accredited university/college as a full-time student.
*All players on a team must play for the same school.
