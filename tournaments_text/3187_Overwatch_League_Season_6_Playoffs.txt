==Information==
===Format===
*'''Playoffs:''' September ''28<sup>th</sup>'' - September ''30<sup>th</sup>''
**Two [[starcraft2:Dual_Tournament_Format|four-team double-elimination groups]]
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Loser picks next map
**<span class="bg-up">Top 2 teams</span> from each group advance to '''Grand Finals'''
*'''Grand Finals:''' October ''1<sup>st</sup>''
**Single elimination bracket
<!--**Semifinals and third place match are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}-->
