==Information==
===Format===
{{box|start|padding=2em}}
====East====
* '''Qualifiers:'''
* Top four Contenders teams from Spring Knockouts play in Summer Qualifiers
** Single Round Robin
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** All teams advance to Stage Knockouts
* '''Knockouts:'''
** Two concurrent 6-team double-elimination brackets
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** ''(Spring)'' <span class="bg-up">Top team</span> from each bracket advances to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Midseason Madness|Midseason Madness]]
** <span class="bg-up">Top 2 teams</span> by average Stage Knockouts finish advance to Playoffs
{{box|break|padding=2em}}
====West====
* '''Qualifiers:'''
* Each team plays eight matches per Stage
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** ''Spring'' 
*** <span class="bg-seedup">Top 2 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Midseason Madness|Midseason Madness]]
*** <span class="bg-up">3rd-6th place teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Spring Stage/Knockouts|Spring Stage Knockouts]] upper bracket
*** <span class="bg-stayup">7th-10th place teams</span> advance to Spring Stage Knockouts lower bracket
** ''Summer''
*** <span class="bg-seedup">Top 3 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Playoffs|Playoffs]]
*** <span class="bg-up">4th-10th place teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Play-Ins|Play-Ins]]
* '''Spring Knockouts:'''
** 8-team double-elimination bracket
** Top 4 teams start in the upper bracket, bottom 4 teams start in the lower bracket
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** <span class="bg-up">Top 2 teams</span> advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 6/Midseason Madness|Midseason Madness]]
{{box|break|padding=2em}}
====Interregion====
* '''Midseason Madness:'''
** 6-team double-elimination bracket
{{box|end}}
