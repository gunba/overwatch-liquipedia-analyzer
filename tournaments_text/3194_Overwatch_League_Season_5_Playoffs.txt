==Information==
===Format===
*Top six teams in the West and three in the East automatically qualify for Playoffs.
*Two teams in the West and one in the East qualify for Playoffs through [[Overwatch League/Season 5/Regular Season/Countdown Cup/Tournament|Countdown Cup]].
*The twelve playoff teams are seeded in a double-elimination bracket
*Seeds 1-4 select their second round opponents.
*{{hero icon/Kiriko}} [[Kiriko]] is available for play.
