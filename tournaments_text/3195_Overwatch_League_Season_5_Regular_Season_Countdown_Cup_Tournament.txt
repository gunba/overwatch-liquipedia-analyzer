==Information==
===Format===
This tournament functions as the play-ins for the [[Overwatch League/Season 5/Playoffs|2022 Overwatch League Playoffs]].
*West
**Teams placed 7-10 in the regular season qualify
**Double elimination bracket 
**Top two teams qualify for the Playoffs.
*East
**Teams placed 4-6 in the regular season qualify
**Single elimination bracket 
**Top team qualifies for the Playoffs.
