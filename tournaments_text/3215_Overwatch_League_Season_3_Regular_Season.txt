==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''':
**Originally scheduled for ''February 8th - August 9th, 2020'', with matches to be played live from various League cities each week ("homestands"). All 20 teams were to play 28 matches over the season.
**Due to the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2019%E2%80%9320_coronavirus_outbreak COVID-19 outbreak], homestands were suspended in Asia at the start of the season and for the rest of the League after Week 5. Games were played exclusively online across two regions -- North America and Asia -- starting in Week 8.
**Midway through the season, a playoff tournament -- the [[/May/Tournament|May Melee]] -- was added, with additional standings wins available for successful teams. The success of this format led to two more tournaments -- the [[/June/Tournament|Summer Showdown]] and the [[/July/Tournament|Countdown Cup]] -- being added thereafter.
**All teams played to '''21''' matches; due to the timing of the outbreak, some teams required several catch-up matches after the Countdown Cup, while some played none. The playoffs then followed in September.
**All matches in the '''Regular Season''' were changed to a '''first-to-three''' format, from the previous one that required at least four maps to be played in all matches. However, League Operations still chose all maps in advance for regular-season matches, while tournaments were played with loser's pick.

====Transfer Window====
The '''Transfer Window''' is defined as the period of time in which Teams can complete trades involving other Teams and/or sign new Players. The Transfer Window is the only time in which these changes can occur following October 30th, 2017. 
* The '''Free Agent Signing Period''' will open on ''January 22nd, 2018''.
* The '''Trade Window''' will open on ''February 11th, 2018''.
* Newly acquired players may be added to a team’s active roster beginning ''February 21st, 2018'', the first day of Stage 2.
* The '''Transfer Window''' closes on ''April 3rd, 2018'', and rosters will lock for the remainder of the season and playoffs.<ref name="Transfer Window">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-gb/news/21498037/reloading-rosters|title=RELOADING ROSTERS|author=Emerald Gao|of=Overwatch League|date=2018-01-26 }}</ref>
