==Information==
The '''Metarama Gaming + Music Festival''' celebrates the worlds of gaming, esports and live music along with comedy, streaming, cosplay and much more. <br>'''Metarama Gaming + Music Festival''' will transform the Las Vegas Festival Grounds on October 19-20, bringing festivalgoers of all ages two full days of entertainment.
