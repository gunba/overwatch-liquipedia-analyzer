==Information==
*'''Postseason (Playoffs)''' - ''August 30th - September 29th, 2019''
**After Stage 4 finishes, the top Atlantic and Pacific division teams, along with the next four teams in the standings regardless of division, will automatically qualify for playoffs.
**The 7th through 12th-place teams in the overall standings will also have a chance to qualify for the playoffs. These teams will be pitted against each other in a '''Single Elimination''' play-in tournament, with the top two teams being able to join the playoffs for a total of eight teams. These final eight teams will compete for the league championship in a '''Double Elimination''' bracket.

===Format===
*[[Patch 1.39]]
*All matches are First-to-4.
*Higher seed picks the first map, all subsequent maps are picked by the team that lost the last map played.
*Maps can only be played once per match.
