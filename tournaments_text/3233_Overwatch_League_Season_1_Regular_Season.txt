==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''January 10th - June 16th, 2018''
**Matches in the Atlantic and Pacific Divisions played out live from the Blizzard Arena each Wednesday through Sunday. Though the season was divided into four stages, every wins and losses from each stage counted towards teams' full-season records and seeding for the postseason. On the last Sunday of each stage, the top teams competed for hefty bonuses and the title of stage winner.
***Stage 1 - ''January 10th - February 10th, 2018'' <ref name="Stage 1 Tickets on Sale Now">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-us/news/21338592/stage-1-tickets-on-sale-now|title=Stage 1 Tickets on Sale Now|author=Blizzard Entertainment |date=2017-12-11}}</ref>
***Stage 2 - ''February 21st - March 25th, 2018'' <ref name="Stage 2 Tickets on Sale Now">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-us/news/21464987/stage-2-tickets-on-sale-now|title=Stage 2 Tickets on Sale Now|author=Blizzard Entertainment |date=2018-01-23}}</ref>
***Stage 3 - ''April 4th - May 6th, 2018'' <ref name="Buy Tickets to Stage 3 and 4 Now">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-us/news/21625846/buy-tickets-to-stage-3-and-4-now|title=Stage 3 and 4 Tickets on Sale Now|author=Blizzard Entertainment |date=2018-03-15}}</ref>
***Stage 4 - ''May 16th - June 17th, 2018'' <ref name="Buy Tickets to Stage 3 and 4 Now">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-us/news/21625846/buy-tickets-to-stage-3-and-4-now|title=Stage 3 and 4 Tickets on Sale Now|author=Blizzard Entertainment |date=2018-03-15}}</ref>
**All matches in the '''Regular Season''' consisted of '''four-map sets'''
**Each Stage of the '''Regular Season''' had its own 8 map pool.
*The top team in each Division, and the next top 4 finishers from the entire league, advanced to the '''Championship Playoffs''' in ''July''.
**The top seed from each Division received a first-round bye.
**Standings tiebreakers:
***Number of match wins;
***Cumulative map differential;
***Head-to-head map differential;
***Head-to-head record;
***Tiebreaker Match;

====Transfer Window====
The '''Transfer Window''' is defined as the period of time in which Teams can complete trades involving other Teams and/or sign new Players. The Transfer Window is the only time in which these changes can occur following October 30th, 2017. 
* The '''Free Agent Signing Period''' opened on ''January 22nd, 2018''.
* The '''Trade Window''' opened on ''February 11th, 2018''.
* Newly acquired players were to be added to a team’s active roster beginning ''February 21st, 2018'', the first day of Stage 2.
* The '''Transfer Window''' closed on ''April 3rd, 2018'', and rosters were locked for the remainder of the season and playoffs.<ref name="Transfer Window">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-gb/news/21498037/reloading-rosters|title=RELOADING ROSTERS|author=Emerald Gao|of=Overwatch League|date=2018-01-26 }}</ref>
