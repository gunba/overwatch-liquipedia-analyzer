==Information==
* The preseason for Season 1 began on December 6th, 2017, where all 12 teams were showcased in exhibition matches.

===Format===
* Exhibition Matches.
* <s>Winners of first round play each other.
* Losers of first round play each other.</s>
** Scratched after Phialdelphia Fusion announced that they would not compete.
