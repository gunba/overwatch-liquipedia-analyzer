==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage #1''': Round Robin 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**3 points awarded for a victory.
**0 points awarded for a defeat.
**If teams are tied on points, it will be broken by their map score, followed by head-to-head results and additional tiebreaker matches.
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group advance to '''Group Stage #2''' (''GSL format'').
**<s>Last placed teams of each group are relegated to the promotion tournament.</s>
**Due to the disband of several teams there will be no promotion tournament. '''Top 5''' teams will advance to [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_4|APEX Season 4]].<ref name="Will be no promotion tournament for Season 4">[http://program.tving.com/ogn/apex/1/Board/View?b_seq=79 Will be no promotion tournament for Season 4]</ref>
*'''Group Stage #2''': GSL format
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group advance to playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''': Single-elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo7}}

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).

*'''Best of 7''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault]] map (excluding map chosen in 3rd set).
**6th Set: [[Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 4th set).
**7th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).

*In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo1}} round will be played on a [[Control]] map.
