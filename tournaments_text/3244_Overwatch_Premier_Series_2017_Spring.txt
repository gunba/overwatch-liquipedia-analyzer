**All matches are Bo3.
**Top 8 advance to '''Playoffs'''.
**Bottom 2 teams are relegated to the promotion tournament ([[Overwatch_Premier_Series/2017/Summer/Preseason|Preseason]]).
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Bo5 with Bo7 Grand Finals.
