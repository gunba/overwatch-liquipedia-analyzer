==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**6 points awarded for a 3:0 victory.
**5 points awarded for a 3:1 victory.
**4 points awarded for a 3:2 victory.
**0 points awarded for a defeat.
**If two teams are tied on points, it will be broken by their head-to-head results.
**If three teams are tied on points, additional tiebreaker matches will be played.
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group advance to playoffs.
**'''Last placed''' teams of each group are relegated to [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 2/Super Week|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 2: Super Week (Promotion)]].
*'''Playoffs''': Single-elimination
**Quarterfinals and Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).

*'''Best of 7''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault]] map (excluding map chosen in 3rd set).
**6th Set: [[Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 4th set).
**7th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).

*In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo1}} round will be played on a [[Control]] map.
