==Information==
===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Group Stage''': Double Elimination
**3 groups for 12 teams.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Top 2 advance to '''Playoffs'''.
**3rd place teams will compete for last 2 spots of '''Playoffs''' in a Round robin qualification bracket.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Quarter- & Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
