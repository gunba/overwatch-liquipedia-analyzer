The 2016 ESL Overwatch Atlantic Showdown is the first major international competition in Overwatch, hosted by ESL and Blizzard. The tournament features eight teams from North America and Europe fighting for the $100,000 prize pool.

==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Duel Tournament
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
