==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #12''' is the 12th European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 


The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/10|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #10]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/11|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #11]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/13|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #13]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/August_2016|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals -August]].
