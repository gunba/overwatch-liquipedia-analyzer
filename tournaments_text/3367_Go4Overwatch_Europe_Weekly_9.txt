==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #9'' is the 9th European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/5|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #5]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/6|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #6]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/7|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #7]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/8|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #8]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/July_2016|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - July]].
