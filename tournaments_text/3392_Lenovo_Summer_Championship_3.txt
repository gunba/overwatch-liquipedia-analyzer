==Overview==
'''Lenovo Overwatch Summer Championship''' is a weekly tournament series hosted by European Gaming League (EGL). Each tournament will see Europe's top teams compete, with the winners of each tournament walking away with €250. Teams will also earn Pro Points based on their placement in each tournament, which will be used to seed all future tournaments.
