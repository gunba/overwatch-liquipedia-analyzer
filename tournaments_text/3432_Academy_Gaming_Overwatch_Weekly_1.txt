==Overview==
The '''Academy Gaming Overwatch Weekly #1''' is the first weekly tournament hosted by Academy Gaming.

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
**All matches {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Round of 16: King's Row -> Dorado -> Watchpoint: Gibraltar (Tiebreaker: Lijiang Tower)
**Round of 8: Hollywood -> Temple of Anubis -> Route 66 (Tiebreaker: Nepal)
**Semifinals and Finals use OWDraft for map drafting on stream.
