==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #82''' is the 82nd European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/79|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #79]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/80|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #80]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/81|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #81]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/83|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #83]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/December_2017|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - December]].
