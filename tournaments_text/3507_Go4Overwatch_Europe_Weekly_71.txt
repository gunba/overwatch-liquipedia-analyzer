==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #71''' is the 71st European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/70|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #70]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/72|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #72]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/73|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #73]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/74|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #74]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/October_2017|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - October]].
