===Format===
*'''Rule set:''' Competitive
*'''High bandwidth:''' On
*'''Game mode:'''
**Start of the tournament - quarterfinals: Best of One
**Quarterfinals & semifinals: Best of Three
**Finals: Best of Five
*'''Map rotation:''' Single map
*'''Return to lobby:''' After a game
*The higher seed starts the map veto
**'''Available maps:''' King's Row, Numbani, Watchpoint: Gibraltar, Dorado, Hanamura, Temple of Anubis, Volskaya, Nepal, Lijiang Tower, Hollywood, Ilios, Route 66, Eichenwalde, Oasis & Horizon Lunar Colony
*'''Hero selection limit:''' 1 per team
**All heroes are currently allowed
*'''Disable kill cam:''' On
