==Overview==
The '''Academy Gaming Overwatch Weekly #128''' is the 128th weekly tournament hosted by [[Academy Gaming]].

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
**All rounds before the round of 16 are {{Abbr/Bo1}}; quarterfinals, semifinals, and final are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Sudden death map of the week: [[Oasis]]
**Round of 32: [[Lijiang Tower]]
**Round of 16: [[King's Row]] -> [[Ilios]] -> [[Dorado]]
**Round of 8: [[Rialto]] -> [[Nepal]] -> [[Hollywood]]
**Semifinals and Finals use OverTown Draft for map drafting on stream.
