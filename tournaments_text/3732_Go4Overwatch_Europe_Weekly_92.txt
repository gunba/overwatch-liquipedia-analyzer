==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #92''' is the 92th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/93|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #93]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/94|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #94]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/95|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #95]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/March_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - March]].
