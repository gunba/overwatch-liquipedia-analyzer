==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage:'''
** 64-team round robin, 16 groups
*** All matches are {{Abbr/Ft2}}
*** <span class="bg-up">Top 1 team</span> per group advance
* '''Playoffs:'''
** 16-team double elimination playoff bracket
*** All matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
** Bracket stop when 8 teams have qualified.
*** <span class="bg-up">Top 8 teams</span> qualify for {{LeagueIconSmall/owc}} [[Overwatch Contenders/2023/Summer Series/Thailand|Contenders Thailand]]
