==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**2 groups of 4 teams.
**All matches are '''4 Game Sets'''.
***Teams with a favorable score based on their won and lost maps, finish first in their Group.
***If two teams win the same number of maps, it will be broken by head-to-head results and a '''Tiebreaker''' {{Abbr/Bo1}}  [[Lijiang Tower]] will be played if necessary.
***If more than two teams win the same number of maps, it will be broken by a '''Tiebreaker''' {{Abbr/Bo1}}  [[Lijiang Tower]] round robin.
**'''Tiebreakers''' will be played after all matches in the '''Group Stage''' are completed.
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
**[[Miraculous Youngster]], [[1246]], [[GC Busan]] and [[RunAway]] advance directly to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.

**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
{{box|break|padding=2em}}
*'''Quarterfinals''':
**Teams directly advanced to playoffs select the first map. 
**[[Lijiang Tower]] will be the '''last''' map and it will be unavailable for both teams to select.
*'''Semifinals''' and '''Finals''':
**[[Lijiang Tower]] will be the '''first''' map and it will be unavailable for both teams to select.
{{box|end}}
*'''Tiebreaker'''
**Directly proceeding to the next game.
**The other side of the team selected the map turns to select the next map in the same type of map pool, if all maps in this type are tied, they are free to select again.
