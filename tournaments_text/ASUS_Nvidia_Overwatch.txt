==About==
'''ASUS Nvidia Overwatch''' is the first Overwatch LAN tournament hosted in Malaysia.  The event was organized and casted by Orange eSports with participation from ASUS and NVIDIA. 

===Format===
* Single elimination {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
