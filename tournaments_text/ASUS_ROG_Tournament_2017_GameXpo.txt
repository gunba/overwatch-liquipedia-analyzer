==About==
===Location===
* '''Playoffs''' take place on the main stage of GameXpo on November 3rd to 5th 2017.

===Game Days===
* Game Days for '''Regular Season''' are Tuesdays and Sundays.

===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''September 19th - October 14th, 2017''
**Seven teams play in a round-robin format.
***All matches are played in a {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
***Top four teams advance to the Playoffs.
***Bottom teams are eliminated.
*'''Playoffs''' - ''November 3rd - November 5th, 2017''
**Four teams play in a single-elimination format over three days.
***Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
***Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
