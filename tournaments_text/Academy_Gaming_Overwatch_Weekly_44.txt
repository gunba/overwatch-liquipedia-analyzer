==Overview==
The '''Academy Gaming Overwatch Weekly #44'' is the 44th weekly tournament hosted by Academy Gaming.

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
**All rounds before the quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo1}}; quarterfinals, semifinals, and final are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Round of 32: Hollywood
**Round of 16: Watchpoint: Gibraltar
**Round of 8:  King's Row -> Numbani -> Hollywood
**Semifinals and Finals use OWDraft for map drafting on stream.
