==Overview==
The '''Academy Gaming Overwatch Weekly #79'' is the 79th weekly tournament hosted by Academy Gaming.

===Format===
*Single Elimination Bracket
**All rounds before the quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo1}}; quarterfinals, semifinals, and final are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Sudden death map of the week: [[Lijiang Tower]]
**Round of 32: [[Nepal]]
**Round of 16: [[King's Row]]
**Round of 8: [[Watchpoint: Gibraltar]] -> [[Numbani]] -> [[Ilios]]
**Semifinals and Finals use OWDraft for map drafting on stream.
