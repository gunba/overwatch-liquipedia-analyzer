'''Agents Fireteam''' is a 3v3 tournament and it is the first of two tournaments in the Agents Overwatch Series organized by Esports Arena.

==Format==
February 9 and February 16 (the qualifying weeks) will each contain two stages, with the Finals held on February 23. 
*Stage One
**12 Round Swiss Tournament.
*Stage Two
**Top 8 teams compete in two groups of 4, with 2 teams from each group advancing to the Week 3 Finals (4 teams advance each week).
*Finals
**Top 4 teams from weeks 1 and 2 compete in a double elimination tournament.
