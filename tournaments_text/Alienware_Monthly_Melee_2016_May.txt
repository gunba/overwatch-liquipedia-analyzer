==Overview==
The first tournament being ran on the release of Overwatch. This tournament will support a new scoring system to determine winners.
* '''Format'''
** KOTH Maps have a 1 Hero Limit
** Capture Point Maps are banned
** {{Abbr/Bo3}} on Day 1
** {{Abbr/Bo5}} on Day 2

* '''Scoring System'''
** Medals based off how quickly a map is completed
*** Gold - 5 Minutes or Less - 3 points
*** Silver - 8 Minutes or Less - 2
*** Bronze - Over 8 Minutes - 1
*** N/A - No completion - 0

* '''Map Pool'''
** ''Winner's Bracket''
** Ro64 - Route 66, Dorado, Ilios
** Ro32 - Numbani, Hollywood, Nepal
** Ro16 - King's Row, Gibraltar, Lijiang Tower
** ''Loser's Bracket''
** R1 - Ilios
** R2 - Lijiang Tower
** R3 - Nepal
** R4 - Lijiang Tower
** R5 - Hollywood, King's Row, Nepal
