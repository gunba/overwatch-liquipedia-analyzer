==Overview==
* '''Due to internet and stream issues, the Lower Bracket Final and Grand Finals had to be rescheduled to May 25, 2017.'''

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**2 Groups of 4 
**Top 2 seeded to Upper Bracket 
**Bottom 2 seeded to Lower bracket 

*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**8 teams 
**Winner's Finals winner gains a one map advantage in the Grand Finals.
