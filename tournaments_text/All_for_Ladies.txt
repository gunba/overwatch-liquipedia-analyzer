==Information==
Organized by Eyedentity Games and Actoz Soft, the “All for Ladies” tournament was the first Korean Overwatch LAN tournament where teams that have only female members could participate.

===Format===
* Playoffs
** Single Elimination
** Round of 32 and Round of 16 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Quaterfinals, Semifinals and Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
