==About==
'''Arène des Héros''' is an Overwatch online tournament organized by [https://facebook.com/lacavalerieoverwatch/ La Cavalerie Overwatch] & [https://lcoesport.gg/ LCO eSport]. 

The tournament was previously known as '''LCO eSport Tournament'''. 


==Information==
This league is divided into two distinct divisions : the '''epic division''' (bronze to platinum) and the '''legendary division''' (diamond to Top 500). The league lasts 6 weeks and offers playoffs to designate two big winners who will each win with the top 3 of each division a definite cashprize win.
===Format===
*'''Stages'''
**Groupe phase and single elimination
**Qualification phases are {{Abbr/Bo3}} on each Saturday
**Semi-finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}} and finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}} on each Sunday

*'''Playoffs'''
**Double elimination
**Finals matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
