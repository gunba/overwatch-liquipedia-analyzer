==Information==
'''Beat Gaming''' announces that the BEAT Invitational Season 2 kicks off July 7th. Teams will battle it out for $6000 USD in their second season tournament.

===Format===
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**Winner's rounds - {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Loser's rounds - {{Abbr/Bo3}}, except Round 3 and Finals - {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Grand Finals - {{Abbr/Bo7}}
