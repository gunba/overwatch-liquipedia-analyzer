==Information==
'''Blackwatch Cup Season 2''' is a Swiss tournament and the second in the Blackwatch Cup series. A series of qualification matches were held online followed by an offline LAN event.

===Format===
*'''Qualifier''': ''February 2<sup>nd</sup> - February 10<sup>th</sup>
**Swiss Format
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**<span class="bg-up">Top 6 teams</span> advance to LAN Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''': ''March 2<sup>nd</sup>
**Double Elimination
**All matches excl. Grand Finals are {{Ft|2}}
**Grand Finals are {{Ft|3}}
