The '''CECC West Qualifier''' was an online regional qualifier in the 2021-22 CECC season, with the winner qualifying for the national finals.

==Information==
===Format===
'''Day 1:''' 5 teams compete in a round robin, first to three format, with the top 4 moving on to the bracket stage.

'''Day 2:''' 4 remaining teams compete in a single elimination bracket.
