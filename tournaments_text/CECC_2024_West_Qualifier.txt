==Information==
*Open to colleges from Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Oregon, Utah, Washington, Wyoming, Alberta, British Columbia, Northwest Territories, and Yukon

===Format===
*'''Group Stage'''
**Four five-team groups
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} from each group advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs'''
**Single-elimination bracket
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 8 teams}} advance to [[CECC/2024/West|West Regional]]
