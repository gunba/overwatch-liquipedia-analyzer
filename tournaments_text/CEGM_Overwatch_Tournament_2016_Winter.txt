===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**Map pool and Point system:
***Type A: Escort and Assaults/Escort maps, winner will get 2 points.
***Type B: Control maps, points according to difference (e.g. team a : team b = 3 : 1, team a will get 2 points)
***Type C: Assaults maps, winner will get 1 point.
***Tie in A and C: Both teams will get 0 point.
**All matches are Bo1.
**Top 4 points earners advance to offline '''Playoffs'''.
**Top 8 points earners get slots in [[CEGM Overwatch Tournament 2017/Spring|2017 Spring]]
*'''Playoffs''':Single Elimination
