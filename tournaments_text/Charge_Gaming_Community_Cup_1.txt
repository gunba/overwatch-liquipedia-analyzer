The '''Charge Gaming Community Cup''' (''short form: CGCC'') is the first of its kind. We combine single elimination with a group phase at the beginning. After each team has played against all the other teams, 8 teams will reach the quarter finals (single elimination). During the group phase, the game is played according to the best-of-3 principle. Best-Of-5 will be played from the quarter finals onwards.
