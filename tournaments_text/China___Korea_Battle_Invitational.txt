==Information==

===Format===
*'''Month''' (Show Matches)
**Single Elimination Showmatch
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**<span class="bg-up">[[Overwatch_Contenders/2020/Season_2/China|Contenders CN Season 2]] '''Week top three (3)'''</span> teams got invited.
*'''Playoff'''
**Single Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**8 teams in the China-Korea playoffs will be divided into two divisions, China Team and Korea Team
**The team that wins each game gets 1 point, and the team with more points after the two rounds of games wins the China-Korea playoffs.
