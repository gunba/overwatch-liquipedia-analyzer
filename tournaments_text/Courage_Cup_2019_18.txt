==Information==

===Format===
* Single Elimination Bracket
* ''Round of 32'' and ''Round of 16'' are {{Abbr/Bo3}}. ''Quarterfinals'' and ''Semifinals'' are {{Abbr/Bo5}}. ''Grand Finals'' are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.

===Map Pool===
*1st Set: [[Blizzard World]]
*2nd Set: [[Assault]] map
*3rd Set: [[Control]] map
*4th Set: [[Escort]] map
*5th Set: [[Control]] map
*'''Tiebreaker''': [[Control]] map

===Qualification===
7 teams of the top 32 were invited directly according to historical achievements (Courage Cup and Open Divisions). 2 teams with the top 2 average rankings among the other 48 teams draw a bye in the qualifier. And the other 23 spots were decided by the qualifier.
