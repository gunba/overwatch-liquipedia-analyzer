==About==
DreamHack Denver 2017 Overwatch was a BYOC LAN event held at Dreamhack Denver 2017. Finals were canceled due to time constraints.

===Format===
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**All games are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
***If Lower Bracket team wins, bracket reset is used, using {{Abbr/Bo3}} as the deciding game.
