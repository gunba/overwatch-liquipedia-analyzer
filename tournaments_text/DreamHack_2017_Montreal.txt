==About==
DreamHack Montreal 2017 Overwatch is a BYOC LAN event held at Dreamhack Montreal 2017.

===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Round Robin
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches until Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Both Finals and Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
*** Winner from Winners' Bracket takes advantage of 1-0 in '''Grand Finals'''.
