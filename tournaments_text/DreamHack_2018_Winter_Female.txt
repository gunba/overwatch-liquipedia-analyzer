==About==
DreamHack Winter 2018 Overwatch Female Tournament is a BYOC LAN event held at DreamHack Winter 2018 for the Female community.

===Format===
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
** Winners' Finals and Losers' Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** All other matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
