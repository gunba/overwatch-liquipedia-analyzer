==About==
DreamHack Summer 2019 Overwatch is a BYOC LAN event held at DreamHack Summer 2019.

===Format===
* '''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
** Quaterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Grand Finals and 3rd place match are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
