==About==
DreamHack Winter 2019 Overwatch is a BYOC LAN event held at Dreamhack Winter 2019.

===Format===
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches until Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Semi Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
*** Winner from Winners' Bracket takes advantage of 1-0 in '''Grand Finals'''.
