==About==

===Format===
*'''Group Stage:''' Round Robin
**Teams are split into six (6) groups of six (6) teams each.
**Matches are played in a First to 3 format.
**<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams from each group</span> advance to Playoffs.
**<span class="bg-stayup">Third place teams</span> face off in the tiebreaker to qualify for Playoffs.
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom three (3) teams</span> are eliminated.
*'''Tie Breaker:''' Round Robin
**One (1) group of six (6) teams.
**Matches are played in a First to 3 format.
**<span class="bg-up">Top four (4) teams</span> advance to Playoffs.
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom two (2) teams</span> are eliminated.
*'''Playoffs:''' Double Elimination
**16 teams in a double elimination bracket.
**Each match, except Grand Finals, will be played in a First to 3 format.
***Grand Finals are played in a First to 4 format.
*The first map is chosen by higher seeding.
*Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
