==About==

===Format===
*Double Elimination
**Top eight (8) teams qualify for [[EBC/2020/EMEA/Regionals/Middle East & Africa|EMEA Regionals Middle East & Africa]].
**Each match will be played in a First to 2 format.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**All maps enabled in competitive play are allowed.
**Game mode rotation: Control, Hybrid, Escort, Assault, Control
