==About==

===Format===
*Double Elimination
**Each match, except Grand Finals, will be played in a First to 3 format.
***Grand Finals are played in a First to 4 format.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
**Top six (6) teams qualify for [[EBC/2020/EMEA/Major|EMEA Major]].
