'''EGoG Overwatch 2 community open cup''' is a tournament organized by Enthusiastic Group of Gamers.

==Information==
===Format===
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**Round Robin with 2 groups, each 8 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}} and played '''Online'''
**'''Top 4 of each group''' advance to the play-offs.
*'''Play-offs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 8 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand-finals are {{abbr/Bo5}} '' (Grand-finals were also played as {{abbr/Bo3}} due to Blizzard-side technical issues)''
**All matches before Winner Finals and Loser Semi-Finals are played '''Online'''
**Winner Finals, Losers Semi- and Finals and Grand Finals are played '''Offline'''
**Offline matches are played in '''Winstrike Arena in Moscow'''
