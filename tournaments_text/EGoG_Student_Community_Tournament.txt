'''EGoG Student Community Tournament''' is a tournament organized by Enthusiastic Group of Gamers.

==Information==
===Format===
* All matches are played '''Online'''
* Only students of the Russian universities are allowed to participate.
* Teams can only be formed from the students of the same university. Each university can have up to two separate teams competing at the same time.
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**Swiss bracket with 6 rounds
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}
**'''Top 8 teams''' advance to the play-offs.
*'''Play-offs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 8 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand-finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}
