The '''eLeague GameStah Spring Series Division 1''' is a weekly league for Oceanic Overwatch teams that qualified across three separate days of swiss format tournaments. Match nights usually take place on Thursdays. After eight rounds, the four teams at the top of the Leaderboard qualify for the league playoffs tournament.

==Information==
===Format===
*'''League Stage''': 8 Week Round Robin
**All matches are Series of 3.
**Wins grant 2 points, draws 1 point and losses 0 points.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**Top 4 teams
