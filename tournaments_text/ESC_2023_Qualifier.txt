==Information==
===Format===
*Open only to ESC members
*Regular Season:
**First map is predetermined, following maps are picked by the loser of the previous map, side picked by the winners
**First map is Control
**Second map is Hybrid
**Third map is Escort
**Fourth map is Push
**Fifth map is Control
**11 week, 12 match round-robin with weeks 5 and 9 as double headers
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 8 teams}} after round robin qualify for playoffs
*All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
*Playoffs:
**Single-elimination bracket
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
**The winner of ESC qualifies for [[CECC/2023|CECC]]
