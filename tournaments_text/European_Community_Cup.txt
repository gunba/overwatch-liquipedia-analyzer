'''European Community Cup''' is a tournament organized by An4rchy.

==Information==
===Format===
*Separate qualifiers to determine top 12 teams
*Round Robin Group Stage with 4 Groups to determine seeding
*Double Elimination Playoffs
*All matches are {{Abbr/Ft2}}, Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
