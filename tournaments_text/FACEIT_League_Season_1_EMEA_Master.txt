==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''April 29<sup>th</sup> - June 9<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Round robin format
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 8 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 14<sup>th</sup> - June 23<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Double elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}, all other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 3 teams}} qualify for {{LeagueIconSmall/esports world cup}} [[Esports World Cup/2024|Esports World Cup 2024]]
**As'''{{team|Spacestation Gaming}}''' and'''{{team|ence}}''' have qualified via {{LeagueIconSmall/overwatch champions series}} [[Overwatch Champions Series/2024/Major|OWCS Major]], the slots will be passed down to the next 3 teams.

===Relegation===
*{{Bgcolortext|stay|1st-12th teams}} remain in Master division for {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league master}} [[FACEIT_League/Season_2/EMEA/Master|Season 2]]
*{{Bgcolortext|staydown|13th-14th place teams}} play in {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 1/EMEA/Relegation|Relegation Matches]]
*{{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 2 teams}} are relegated to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/EMEA/Expert|Season 2 Expert]]
