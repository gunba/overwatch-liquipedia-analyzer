==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''April 29<sup>th</sup> - June 9<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**12 Swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 8 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 10<sup>th</sup> - June 30<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Double elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}, all other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}

===Promotion/Relegation===
*{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} are promoted to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league master}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/NA/Master|Season 2 Master]]
*{{Bgcolortext|stayup|3rd-4th place teams}} play in {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 1/NA/Relegation|Relegation Matches]]
*{{Bgcolortext|down|Teams with less than 4 wins}} are relegated to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/NA/Open|Season 2 Open]]
