==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''April 29<sup>th</sup> - June 9<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Round robin format
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 14<sup>th</sup> - June 16<sup>th</sup> 2024''
**Double elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}, all other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Winner}} qualifies for {{LeagueIconSmall/esports world cup}} [[Esports World Cup/2024|Esports World Cup 2024]]

===Relegation===
*{{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 2 teams}} are relegated to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/OCE/Open|Season 2 Open]]
