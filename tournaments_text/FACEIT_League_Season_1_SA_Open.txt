==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''May 3<sup>rd</sup> - May 26<sup>th</sup>, 2024''
**Round robin format
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 10<sup>th</sup> - June 23<sup>rd</sup> 2024''
**Double elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}, all other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}

===Promotion===
*{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} are promoted to {{LeagueIconSmall/faceit league master}} [[FACEIT League/Season 2/SA/Master|Season 2 Master]]
