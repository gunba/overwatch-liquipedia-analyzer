==About==

The winner of the Cup is crowned the Finnish Champion because it is an official eSM competition.<br>
'''Grand Finals''' were played 2019-06-25 in [https://www.finnkino.fi/en/ Finnkino, Helsinki]

===Format===
*'''Round Robin:'''
**{{Abbr/Bo5}} matches
**<span class="bg-up">First team</span> advance to the '''Grand Finals'''.
**<span class="bg-stayup">2nd to 5th</span> will play in the playoffs for a place in '''Grand Finals'''.
