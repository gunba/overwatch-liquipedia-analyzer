==Information==
*'''Qualifier''':
**'''Group Stage''': ''November 18<sup>th</sup>, 2023''
***Groups of 8 teams
***Round robin matches
***{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} from each group advance to Bracket Stage
**'''Bracket Stage''': ''November 19<sup>th</sup>, 2023''
***Eight single-elimination brackets
***{{Bgcolortext|up|Winner}} of each bracket advances to Knockouts

**Double elimination bracket
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
