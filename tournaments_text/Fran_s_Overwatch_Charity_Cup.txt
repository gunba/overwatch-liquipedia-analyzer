'''Fran's Overwatch Charity Cup''' is a community event organized by Overwatch player/streamer Fran and broadcast by Monkey Bubble.

==Information==
===Format===
* '''General Rules'''
**Competitive mode
**Single elimination
**Matches are Bo3 and finals are Bo5
**The map order is as follows: Control → Escort → Hybrid → Control.
**For finals the map order changes to: Control → Escort → Hybrid → Assault → Control.
**Any map may only be played once during the match.
