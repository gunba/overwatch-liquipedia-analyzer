===About===

The '''FrostWatch''' is a French tournament organized by '''Frost Tails'''. 

===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Swiss Format
** 1 match par week.
** 4 pools of 4 teams randomized.
*** Top eight (8) teams advance to the Playoffs Winner Bracket.
*** Low eight (8) teams advance to the Playoffs Loser Bracket.

* '''Playoff''' : Double Elimination Bracket
** Bo3
