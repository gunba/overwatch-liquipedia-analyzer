==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #101''' is the 101st European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/102|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #102]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/103|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #103]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/104|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #104]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/May_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - May]].
