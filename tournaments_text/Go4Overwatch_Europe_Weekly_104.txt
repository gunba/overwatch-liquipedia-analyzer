==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #104''' is the 104th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/101|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #101]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/102|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #102]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/103|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #103]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/May_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - May]].
