==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #17''' is the 17th European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/14|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #14]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/15|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #15]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/16|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #16]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/September_2016|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals -September]].
