==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #18''' is the 18th European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/19|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #19]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/20|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #20]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/21|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #21]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/22|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #22]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/October_2016|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals -October]].
