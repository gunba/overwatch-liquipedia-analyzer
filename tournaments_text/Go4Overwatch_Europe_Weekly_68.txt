==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #68'' is the 68th European installment of ESL Go4 cups. 

The Go4-Points earned at this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/66|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #66]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/67|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #67]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/69|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #69]]  to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/September_2017|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - September]].
