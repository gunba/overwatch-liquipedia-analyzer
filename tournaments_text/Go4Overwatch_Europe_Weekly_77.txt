==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #77''' is the 77th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/75|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #75]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/76|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #76]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/78|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #78]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/November_2017|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - November]].
