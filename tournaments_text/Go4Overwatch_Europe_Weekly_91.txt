==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #91''' is the 91th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/88|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #88]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/89|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #89]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/90|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #90]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/February_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - February]].
