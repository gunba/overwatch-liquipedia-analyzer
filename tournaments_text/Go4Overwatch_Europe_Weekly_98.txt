==Information==
'''ESL Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #98''' is the 98th European installment of ESL Go4 cups.

The Go4-Points earned by this Tournament add up with [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/96|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #96]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/97|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #97]], [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/99|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #99]] and [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Weekly/100|Go4Overwatch Europe Cup #100]] to qualify for [[Go4Overwatch/Europe/Monthly/April_2018|Go4Overwatch Europe Monthly Finals - April]].
