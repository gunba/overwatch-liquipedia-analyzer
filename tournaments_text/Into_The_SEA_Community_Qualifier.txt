==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Single Elimination
** Teams are split into 4 groups
** Each group will play a single-elimination bracket, all matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** The top team of each group proceeds to Playoffs
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** The top 4 teams will play a double-elimination bracket
** All matches except Grand Finals will be {{Abbr/Bo5}}, Grand Finals will be {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
