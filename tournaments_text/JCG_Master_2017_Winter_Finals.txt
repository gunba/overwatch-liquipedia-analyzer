==Information==
'''JCG Master 2016-2017 Winter Finals''' is a [[:Category:Asia|Japanese]] tournament.

*Gran Finals - Grand Finals: Tokyo School of Anime, Kita Kasai Schoolhouse 5F, Tokyo.

===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**Al matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}} [''Online'']
**Semifinals and Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}} [''Online'']
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}} [''Offline'']
