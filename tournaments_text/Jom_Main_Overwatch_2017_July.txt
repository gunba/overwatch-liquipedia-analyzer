==Overview==
It's been a great ride so far for all of us at [[Midnight Gaming]] and this time around we're very lucky to have Acer come on as our Supporting Partner. 

As usual, team registrations will be on a first come first serve basis. 8 teams only.

LET'S GO!

===Format===
'''Game Rules & Settings'''
* '''Rule set''': Competitive
* '''Game mode''':
** Pre-Semifinals: Best of Five
** Semifinals and Finals: Best of Seven
* '''Map rotation''': Single map

'''Map Selection'''
* '''Best of 5''':
** 1st Set: [[Control]] map.
** 2nd Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
** 3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
** 4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
** 5th Set: [[Hybrid]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).

* Best of 7:
** 1st Set: [[Control]] map.
** 2nd Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
** 3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
** 4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
** 5th Set: [[Assault]] map (excluding map chosen in 3rd set).
** 6th Set: [[Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 4th set).
** 7th Set: [[Hybrid]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).
