'''KMA Esports Summer Fest''' is a tournament organized by KMA.

==Information==
===Format===
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**Double Elimination bracket
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}
**'''Top 4 teams''' advance to the play-offs each qualifier
*'''Play-offs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 8 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand-finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}
