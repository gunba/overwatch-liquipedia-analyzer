==Overview==
'''King of the Watch Europe #1''' is the second installment, and the first in Europe, of a King of the Hill series from Creation eSports.

{{prize pool end}}

===Format===
*Up to three {{Abbr/Bo3}} matches
**Winner of two {{Abbr/Bo3}} matches wins
**Each team Attacks and Defends, the quickest time wins
**Challenger selects the first map, loser picks the next map.
