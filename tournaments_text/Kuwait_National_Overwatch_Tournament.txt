==Information==
'''Kuwait National Overatwch Tournament''', or short KNOT, is a [[Minor Tournaments|minor]] tournament, organized by "ExtravaGaming".

===Format===
*'''Bracket''': Single Elimination
**Round of 16 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Quarterfinals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Semifinals and Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
