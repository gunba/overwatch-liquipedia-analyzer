==About==
LANime Matsuri: Overwatch was a side event held during the Anime Matsuri Japanese Culture Convention

===Format===
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**All games are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
***If Lower Bracket team wins, bracket reset is used, using {{Abbr/Bo5}} as the deciding game.
