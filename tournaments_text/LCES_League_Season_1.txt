==Information==

===Format===
*'''Qualifiers''': 2 stages
**All matches are '''{{Abbr/Bo3}}'''.
**Top 4 teams from each stage advance to the Group Stage.
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**12 teams are divided into 2 groups.
**All matches consist of '''4-map sets'''.
**Playoffs are held in '''{{Abbr/Bo5}}''' and '''{{Abbr/Bo7}}'''(finals) formats.
