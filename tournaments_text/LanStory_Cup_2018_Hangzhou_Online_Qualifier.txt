==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage:'''
**Round robin for 2 groups.
**Top two teams of each group advance to playoffs.
* '''Playoffs:'''
**Single Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Top 3 teams of playoffs advance to offline [[LanStory Cup/2018/Hangzhou|main event]] in Hangzhou.
