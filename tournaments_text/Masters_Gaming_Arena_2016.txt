===Format===
*'''Qualifier''':
**2 Days Regional Online Qualifier running in 3 Regions (Americas, Europe & Asia) with up to 128 teams.
**Top 8 teams from each Regional Online Qualifier will proceed to Regional Final and ONE (1) winning team from each Region will represent their region and earn their team a ticket to the Grand Finals.
**3 Regional winning teams and 1 MSI sponsored team will compete at Grand Final for the champion title.
*'''Main Event''': Double-elimination
**All matches are Bo5.
**Grand Finals are Bo7.
