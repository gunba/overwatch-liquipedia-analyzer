==About==
Montreal Overwatch League is a local gaming movement designed to make the Overwatch community grow in Montreal and Canada.

Our primary goal is to share our love of gaming and bring gamers from Montreal (and surrounding areas) into a dynamic, accessible and competitive environment.

==Tournament Information==
===Format===
* '''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
**Map change each week
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Top 8 teams advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
