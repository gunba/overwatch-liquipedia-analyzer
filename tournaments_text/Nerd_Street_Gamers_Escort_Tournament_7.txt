==Information==
===Format===
*Up to 16 teams in a '''Double Elimination''' format.
*Matches will be played in a '''First to 2''' format.
*Grand Finals will be played in a '''First to 4''' format.
*First map is picked by the higher seeded team.
*All games will be private matches on the Escort Game Mode using the maps stated in our map pool.
