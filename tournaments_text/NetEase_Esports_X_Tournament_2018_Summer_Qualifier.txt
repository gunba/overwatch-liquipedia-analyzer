==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage:'''
**Round robin for 4 groups
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**Winner of each group advances to '''Playoffs'''
* '''Playoffs:'''
**Single Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Top two teams of '''Playoffs''' advance to [[NetEase_Esports_X_Tournament/2018/Summer|Main Event]]
***Because of [[Lucky Future Zenith]] dropping from the tournament, a third team will also qualify.<ref name="LFZ pullout">{{cite web|url=https://www.weibo.com/6613329781/GzXT3lMHD?type=comment|title=LFZ Pullout of tournament|date=2018-09-18|publisher=NetEase Esports}}</ref>
