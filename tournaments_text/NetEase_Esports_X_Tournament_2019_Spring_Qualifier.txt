==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
**'''Top 1''' teams of each group advance to '''[[NetEase_Esports_X_Tournament/2019/Spring|Main Event]]'''
** '''2nd''' and '''3rd''' teams of each group draw lots to decide their opponents in '''Knockout Stage'''
**Last placed teams of each group are eliminated
*'''Knockout Stage''': Single Elimination.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
**Winners advance to '''[[NetEase_Esports_X_Tournament/2019/Spring|Main Event]]'''
