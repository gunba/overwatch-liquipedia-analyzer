==About==
===Nevermore Esports League===
Season 1 is the inaugural Overwatch season for Nevermore Esports League. The league features over 20 teams with players picked by captains during the preseason.
===Format===
* Preseason
** Three weeks in length
** Recruiting and roster changes allowed
** Tryouts for new players
** At the end of the preseason, rosters are locked

* Regular Season
** Each team has one match per week
** All teams will play each other once
** All matches in the Regular Season will consist of four-map sets
** Three divisions are made based purely off of average SR of the team

* Playoffs
**Tournament matches will be split across three divisions. The first division will be a combination of Division 1 and Division 2 teams that completed Season One. Division 2 will be composed of teams from Division 2 and Division 3 that completed Season One. Lastly, Division 3 will be an open division of teams that registered for Season One, played at least one match, but had to drop out of the season due to roster issues, and our rules that did not allow them to add additional new players once the season started.
** Bo5
