===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**Top 2 of each group advance to Winners' Bracket of '''Playoffs'''.
**Bottom 2 of each group advance to Losers' Bracket of '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**Quarterfinals - Winners' Finals: Bo3
**Losers' Finals: Bo5
**Grand Finals: Bo7
**Winner from winner's bracket takes advantage of 1:0 in Grand Finals.
