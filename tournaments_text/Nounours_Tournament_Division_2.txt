==Information==
===Format===
*'''Swiss Stage''': September 16<sup>th</sup> - 17<sup>th</sup>, 2023
**Five Swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Abbr/Ft2}}
**Splitting teams in 2 groups according of Swiss Round Ranking (1st Group: Top 1-15 / 2nd Group: Top 16-30)
**1st Group advances to playoffs in [[Nounours_Tournament/Division_1|Division 1]]
**2nd Group advances to playoffs in [[Nounours_Tournament/Division_2|Division 2]]
*'''Playoffs''': September 23<sup>rd</sup> - October 1<sup>st</sup>, 2023
**Double elimination bracket
**Matches up to Semifinals are {{Abbr/Ft2}}
**Upper and Lower Finals are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}
