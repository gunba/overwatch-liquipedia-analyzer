==About==
'''Open Division - Korea''' is a [[:Category:Asia|Korean]] tournament played in cities such Seoul, Kangreung, DaeJeon, GwangJu, Busan...
===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Qualifier''': Regional qualifiers
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Quarter Finals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand & Semi Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.

===Map Pool===

*'''Best of 3''':
**1st Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Escort]] map.

*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).

*In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo1}} round will be played on a [[Control]] map.
