===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Double Elimination
**Group '''A''' to '''F''' of 8 teams, Group '''G''' to '''J''' of 7 teams.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**'''Top 1''' of each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Quarter and Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
