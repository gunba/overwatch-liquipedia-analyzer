==Information==
===Format===
* '''Hard League''': Swiss Format
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Top ten (10) teams advance to the Playoffs of the [[Open_Division/2020/Season_3/Korea#Playoffs|Open Division 2020 Season 3 - Korea]].
** Bottom teams are eliminated.
** A four-week run
* '''Easy League''': Single Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Semifinals and Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Top two (2) teams advance to the Playoffs of the [[Open_Division/2020/Season_3/Korea#Playoffs|Open Division 2020 Season 3 - Korea]].
** A week's run
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}, except Grand Final that is {{Abbr/Bo7}}
*** Winners' bracket advantage of 1 game.
