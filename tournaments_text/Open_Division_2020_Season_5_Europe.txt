==Information==
===Format===
* '''Regular Bracket''': Double Elimination
** Round 1 and Round 2 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Winners' Round 3 to Winners' Semifinals and Losers' Round 3 to Losers' Round 11 are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Top six (6) teams qualify for Final Bracket.
*** Winners' Finals, Losers' Seminfinals, Losers' Finals and Grand Finals are not played.
* '''Final Bracket''': Double Elimination
** Top six (6) teams from Regular bracket face off against the five (5) teams, that dropped from Contenders Trials in October.
** Trial's teams are seeded 1-5, and the top Open Division teams are seeded 6-11. Performance and SR is accounted for to determine each group's seeds.
** Round 1 and Round 2 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Winners' Semifinals and Losers' Round 3 to Losers' Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
** Winners' Final and Grand Final are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
*** Winners' bracket advantage of 1 game.
** Top four (4) teams receive invitations for Contenders Trials in November.
