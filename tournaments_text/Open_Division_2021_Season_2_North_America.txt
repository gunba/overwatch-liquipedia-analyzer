==Information==
===Format===
* Both Winner's & Losers' Round 1 and Round 2 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
* Winners' Round 3 to Winners' Semifinals and Losers' Round 3 to Losers' Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
* Winners' Finals and Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}.
** Winners' bracket advantage of 1 game in the Grand Finals
* Top two (2) teams receive invitations for Contenders Trials in May.
