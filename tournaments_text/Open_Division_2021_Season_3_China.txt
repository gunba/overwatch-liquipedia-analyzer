==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Swiss Format
** All matches are '''first-to-3'''.
** Top eight (8) teams advance to the Playoffs of the [[Open_Division/2021/Season_3/China|Open Division 2021 Season 3 - China]].
** Bottom teams are eliminated.
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches are  '''first-to-3''', except Grand Final that is  '''first-to-4'''.
*** Winners' bracket advantage of 1 game.
