==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': Swiss Format
** All matches are {{Abbr|Ft3}}
** Top eight (8) teams advance to the Playoffs of the [[Open_Division/2022/Season_1/China|Open Division 2022 Season 1 - China]].
** Bottom teams are eliminated.
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** All matches are  {{Abbr|Ft3}}, except Grand Final that is  {{Abbr|Ft4}}.
*** Winners' bracket advantage of 1 game.
