==Notes==
<section begin="Notes"/>
[[Team Hush]] was disqualified for having an alleged Korean Contenders player ([[Shubil]] - needs confirmation) smurf on a platinum account; the discrepancy was brought to light from one of [[Team Hush]]'s members' stream. Upon accusations, they refused the admins' request to show the mentioned stream. Team [[RK House]] was forced to finish their match against Team Hush despite the accusations already being dealt with by the admins, and [[JayCoBird]] was given a default win after admins decided to disqualify [[Team Hush]]. It should be noted that [[RK House]] was not given any chance to replay their rightful bracket against [[JayCoBird]] despite the ruling to disqualify [[Team Hush]].

[[RK House]]'s loss was surrounded by controversy after the qualifiers; not only were they forced to finish their match against [[Team Hush]] even with ongoing accusations of smurfing - which was explicitly highlighted in the ACE rulebook, they did not have a chance to fight [[JayCoBird]] after [[Team Hush]]'s disqualification. Said disqualification was decided halfway through [[Team Hush]] / [[JayCoBird]]'s match and the decisive win was given to [[JayCoBird]]. Multiple participants of the ACE tournament had brought up the missing match between [[JayCobird]] and [[RK House]], but no concrete answer was provided. It is speculated that [[RK House]] lost to [[FES Bhava]] due to the lack of admin interference in the [[RK House]]/[[Team Hush]] match, which was played to 2-3 and lasted nearly 2 hours. The admin team has yet to address [[RK House]] regarding this controversy, despite repeated requests from its members.
