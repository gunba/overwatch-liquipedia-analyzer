===Format===
*Eight teams will participate in the group stage, comprising the top five Teams from the
[[Overwatch ACE/2020/Open Qualifier|Qualifiers]] and three invited teams.
*'''Regular Season:'''
**Round Robin (4 maps format) 
**<span class="bg-up">Top Four (4)</span> advance to the '''[[Overwatch_ACE/2020#Playoffs|Playoffs]]'''
*'''Championship:'''
**Single Elimination
