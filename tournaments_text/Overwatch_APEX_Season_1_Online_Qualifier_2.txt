==About==
The '''Overwatch APEX Season 1: Online Qualifier #2''' is a open qualifier tournament that will seed 56 teams into [[Overwatch APEX/Season 1/Offline Qualifier/2|Offline Qualifier #2]]

===Format===
*Online Qualifiers: October 29, 2016 to October 30, 2016.
*Teams will be split into 8 double-elimination brackets with 12 teams each.
**'''Top 6''' teams from each bracket will advance to Offline Qualifier #2.
*The three losing teams in Losers' Round 2 will be placed in a Wildcard round-robin.
**'''Winner''' of each Wildcard group will advance to Offline Qualifier #2.
