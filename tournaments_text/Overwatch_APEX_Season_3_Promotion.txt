==About==
===Format===
*4 teams from [[Overwatch APEX/Season 2|Overwatch APEX Season 2]].
*4 teams from [[Overwatch APEX/Challengers/Season 3|Overwatch APEX Challengers Season 3]].
*'''Group Stage''': GSL format.
**The top 4 challenger teams will choose their opponents, in descending order based on their APEX Points.
**The remaining teams are allocated into groups randomly. 
**'''Top 2''' teams of each group qualify for [[Overwatch APEX/Season 3|Overwatch APEX Season 3]].
*'''Losers Groups''': GSL format
**Bottom two teams from each group play in final group to determine two more qualifiers for[[Overwatch APEX/Season 3|Overwatch APEX Season 3]].

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**4th Set: [[Escort]] map.
**5th Set: [[Assault/Escort]] map (excluding map chosen in 2nd set).
