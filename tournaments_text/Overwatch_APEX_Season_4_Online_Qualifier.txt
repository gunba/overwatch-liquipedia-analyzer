==About==
The '''Overwatch APEX Season 4: Online Qualifier''' is a single-elimination tournament that will seed 14 teams into [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_4/Offline Qualifier|Offline Qualifier]]

===Format===
*'''General rules''': Competitive mode.
*'''Bracket''': Single Elimination.
**'''Top 24 teams''' adanvance to '''Group Stage'''.
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin.
**4 groups; each group has 3 teams.
***Second placed team of each group advances to '''Wildcard'''.
***'''Top team''' of each group advances to [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_4/Offline Qualifier|Offline Qualifier]].
*'''Wildcard''': Round Robin.
**2 groups; each group has 4 teams.
***'''Top 3 teams''' of each group advance to [[Overwatch_APEX/Season_4/Offline Qualifier|Offline Qualifier]].
