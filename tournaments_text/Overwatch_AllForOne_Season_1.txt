==About==

===Format===
*'''Regular Season''' - ''February 25th - June, 2019''
**The teams will be splitted in 7 Divisions depending of their level. 
***Cycle 1 - ''February 25th - April 14th, 2019''
***Cycle 2 - ''April 29th - June 16th, 2019''
*'''Postseason (Playoffs)''' - ''June, 2019''

====Transfer Window====
The '''Transfer Window''' is defined as the period of time in which Teams can complete trades involving other Teams and/or sign new Players. The Transfer Window is the only time in which these changes can occur.
* The '''Transfer Window''' will open on ''April 15th, 2019''.
* The '''Transfer Window''' closes on ''April 28th, 2019'', and rosters will lock for the remainder of the season and playoffs.
