==About==
'''Overwatch Arena Clash''' is a grassroots esports tournament run up and down the UK. Teams in '''BELONG Gaming Arenas''' nationwide, battle it out to become the '''Arena Clash''' champions.
===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Group Stage''': 
**All matches are played in a four (4) maps format (Overwatch League Format):
***If both teams tie a map, tiebreaker will '''not''' be played and the tie map will not be shown on the scoreboard;
***If both teams tie the series (2-2), a fifth map will be played ([[Control]])
**Top two (2) teams from each group advance to the playoffs'''
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination, Bo5 matches
*'''Grand Finals''': Offline Bo5 match held on stage at Insomnia64

===Special Awards===
*'''Tournament MVP''': [[Padshack]]. A long time participant in the '''OAC''',  "[[Padshack]]"  started playing for the '''[[Pirates]]''' team when the tournament first began. Since then he has moved to the '''[[Spartans]]''' and been a key member of their roster. One of the strongest DPS players in the tournament, he is known as a highly passionate player who constantly strives to improve both his team's, and his own game play. Having been pipped to the post time and time again to win the tournament, the season he was finally able to bring it home is a very fitting place to award him the MVP.


*'''Community Champion''': [[Ruggy Ram]]. Having been in the '''OAC''' from the start as well, Zach "[[Ruggy Ram]]" Grace of the '''[[Steelers]]''' is a motivated member of the community. He is dedicated to bringing the players together and allowing them to enjoy PUG nights, and other activities. He has organised the '''OAC''' All Stars Show match and it's a brilliant part of the '''Arena Clash''' family.
