===Format===
*'''Group Stage:''' ''March 1<sup>st</sup> - 24<sup>th</sup>''
**Round robin group
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} advance to Seeding Decider Matches
**{{Bgcolortext|stayup|Next 4 teams}} advance to Last Chance Qualifier
**{{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom team}} is eliminated
*'''Seeding Decider Matches:''' ''March 29<sup>th</sup> - 31<sup>st</sup>''
**Round robin group
**{{Bgcolortext|up|All 4 teams}} advance to Playoffs
**{{Bgcolortext|byeup|Top 2 teams}} receive a first-round bye
*'''Last Chance Qualifier:''' ''March 28<sup>th</sup> - 31<sup>st</sup>''
**Double elimination bracket
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} advance to Playoffs
*'''Playoffs:''' ''April 5<sup>th</sup> - 7<sup>th</sup>''
**Single elimination bracket
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 3 teams}} advance to {{LeagueIconSmall/owcs}} [[Overwatch Champions Series/2024/Asia/Stage 1/Main Event|Stage 1 Main Event]]
**{{Bgcolortext|bg-fourth|Fourth place team}} advances to {{LeagueIconSmall/owcs}} [[Overwatch Champions Series/2024/Asia/Stage 1/Wild Card|Wild Card]]
