==Information==
===Format===
===Format===
*'''Swiss:''' ''August 9<sup>th</sup> - 11<sup>th</sup>''
**Nine swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 24 teams}} (or all 7-2 teams, whichever is more) advance to Top Cut Swiss
*'''Top Cut Swiss:''' ''August 11<sup>th</sup>''
**Two swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 16 teams}} advance to Group Stage
*'''Group Stage:''' ''August 16<sup>th</sup> - August 25<sup>th</sup>''
**Four GSL groups
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} from each group advance to [[Overwatch Champions Series/2024/EMEA/Stage 3|Main Event]]
