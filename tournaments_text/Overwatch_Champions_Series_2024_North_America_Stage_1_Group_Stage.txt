===Format===
*'''Swiss:''' ''March 1<sup>st</sup> - 3<sup>rd</sup>''
**Nine swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 16 teams}} advance to Group Stage

**Four GSL groups
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} from each group advance to [[Overwatch Champions Series/2024/North America/Stage 1/Main Event|Main Event]]
