==Information==
*'''{{Hero|venture}}''' is ''not'' available for play.
===Format===
*'''Swiss:''' ''April 5<sup>th</sup> - 7<sup>th</sup>''
**Nine swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 24 teams}} (or all 7-2 teams, whichever is more) advance to Top Cut Swiss
*'''Top Cut Swiss:''' ''April 7<sup>th</sup>''
**Two swiss rounds
**All matches are {{Ft|2}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 16 teams}} advance to Group Stage
*'''Group Stage:''' ''April 12<sup>th</sup> - 21<sup>st</sup>''
**Four GSL groups
**All matches are {{Ft|3}}
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} from each group advance to [[Overwatch Champions Series/2024/North America/Stage 2/Main Event|Main Event]]
