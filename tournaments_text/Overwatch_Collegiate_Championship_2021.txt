==Information==
===Format===
*National Swiss
**Teams entered into the National Swiss Tournament will participate in 10 rounds of games in a Swiss-Offset format.
**Each match is a first to 2 wins.
**Winners of the National Swiss will move onto the Seeding mixer. 
**All teams regardless of Varsity status are eligible to compete in the National Swiss. 

*Varsity Wildcard 
**Varsity teams that do not place in the top 24 of the National Swiss will be invited to participate in the Varsity Wildcard bracket.
**Each match is a first to 2 wins.
**The number of teams progressing out of this stage will be based on the number of Buy-In Teams. For each buy in team that progresses to Seeding Mixer, one less team from the Varsity Wildcard will progress to the seeding mixer. 
**The minimum number of teams that will be seeded through the Varsity Wildcard is 4 teams.
**Buy-In Teams 
***Teams that are a part of our Partner leagues will join the Varsity Wildcard winners and move into the Seeding mixer. For each Buy-In team that makes it into the Seeding Mixer, one less team will be seeded through the Varsity Wildcard.
***The maximum number of teams that will be seeded as a Buy-In Team is 4 teams.

*Seeding Mixer 
**The seeding mixer will be a single-elimination tournament that will aid in producing seeding values for Playoffs.
**The winner of the 7-3 bracket and all teams in the 9-1 and 8-2 brackets advance to the National Championship

*Playoffs
**The Playoffs will be a single-elimination where the teams will compete for tournament placement and prizing.
**Seeding is based on the National Swiss and Seeding mixer.
