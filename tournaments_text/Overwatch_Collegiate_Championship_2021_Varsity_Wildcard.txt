==Format==
*Varsity Wildcard 
**Varsity teams that do not place in the top 24 of the National Swiss will be invited to participate in the Varsity Wildcard bracket.
**Each match is a first to 2 wins.
**Top 7 teams from the Varsity Wildcard advance to the [[Overwatch Collegiate Championship/2021/National Championship|National Championship]]
