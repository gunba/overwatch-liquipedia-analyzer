==Information==
===Format===
<section begin="Swiss"/>
*Swiss
**Regional groups
**Each match is first to 2
**Top 32 teams from each region move on to Group Stage
<section end="Swiss"/>
<section begin="Groups"/>
*Group Stage 
**Eight interregional round robin groups
**Each match is first to 2
**Top 2 teams from each group advance to Playoffs.
<section end="Groups"/>
*Playoffs
**Double elimination
**Seeding is based on the Swiss and Groups performance.
