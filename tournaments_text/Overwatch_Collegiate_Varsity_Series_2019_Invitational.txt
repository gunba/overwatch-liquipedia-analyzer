==Information==
===Format===
* '''Invitational''' Sixteen (16) teams split into two groups of round-robin: East and West.
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*** Top eight (8) teams advance to the [[Tespa Overwatch Collegiate Varsity Series 2019/Playoffs|Playoffs]].
*** Bottom eight (8) teams fall to the [[Tespa_Overwatch_Collegiate_Varsity_Series_2019/Main_Season#Play-Ins_Top_8|Play-Ins]].
