==Information==
===Format===
* '''Second Chance Tournament''' All teams who failed to qualify in the Qualifiers may take part in this tournament for seeding into the Play Ins.
** Teams will play eight (8) rounds of Swiss.
*** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
* '''Play-ins''' 
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*** Top two (2) teams advance to the [[Tespa Overwatch Collegiate Varsity Series 2019/Playoffs|Playoffs]].
