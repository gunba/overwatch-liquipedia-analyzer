==Information==
<!--===Format===
* '''Play-Ins''' 
** Single elimination.
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*** Top two (2) teams advance to the [[Overwatch Collegiate Varsity Series/2019/Invitational|Varsity Invitational]].
* '''Invitational''' 
** Sixteen (16) teams split into two groups of round-robin: East and West.
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*** Top eight (8) teams advance to the [[Overwatch Collegiate Varsity Series/2019#Playoffs|Playoffs]].
*** Bottom eight (8) teams fall to the [[Overwatch_Collegiate_Varsity_Series/2019/Main_Season#Play-Ins_Top_8|Play-Ins]].
* '''Second Chance Tournament''' 
** All teams who failed to qualify in the Qualifiers may take part in this tournament for seeding into the Play Ins.
** Teams will play eight (8) rounds of Swiss.
*** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
* '''Play-ins''' 
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*** Top two (2) teams advance to the [[Overwatch Collegiate Varsity Series/2019#Playoffs|Playoffs]].
* '''Playoffs'''
** Eight (8) teams compete in Towers of Doom brackets. 
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
