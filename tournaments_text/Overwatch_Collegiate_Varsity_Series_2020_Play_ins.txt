==Information==
===Format===
* '''Play-ins''' 
** Consists of bottom sixteen (16) teams from the Invitational and the top forty-eight (48) teams from the Second-Chance Season
** Double-elimination bracket
** First-to-two format
** Top four (4) teams qualify for the Playoffs
