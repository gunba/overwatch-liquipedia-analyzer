==Information==
'''Contenders 2018 Season 1: Atlantic Showdown''' is a Show Match that takes place after the [[Overwatch Contenders/2018/Season 1/Europe/Playoffs|Europe]] and [[Overwatch_Contenders/2018/Season_1/North_America|North American]] playoffs.  The winner of each faces off against each other in a '''best-of-five Show Match'''.
