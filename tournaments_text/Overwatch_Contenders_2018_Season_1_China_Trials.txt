==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Single Round Robin
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top3''' advance to '''Playoff Stage'''.
**[[JD-Gaming]], [[Moss Seven Club]] and [[Team_Skadis_Gift/SKG|SKG]] directly advance to '''Playoff Stage'''.
*'''Playoff Stage''': Single Round Robin
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**<s>'''Top 3''' advance to [https://liquipedia.net/overwatch/Overwatch_Contenders/2018/Season_1/China Contenders 2018 Season 1: China].</s>
***Because of teams dropping their rosters and pulling out of the tournament, '''Top 5''' advance to [https://liquipedia.net/overwatch/Overwatch_Contenders/2018/Season_1/China Contenders 2018 Season 1: China].
