==Information==
Top teams from Europe, North America, and South America will meet in a double-elimination showdown between May 31–June 2.
Invitations will be sent out to:  
*Champion of [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Season_1/North_America_East|Contenders North America East]]
*Champion of [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Season_1/North_America_West|Contenders North America West]]  
*The final spot for North America will be decided via a [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Atlantic_Showdown_Tiebreaker|tiebreaker match]] between the runner-ups of '''North America East''' and '''North America West'''.
*Champion of [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Season_1/South_America|Contenders South America]]
*Finalists of [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Season_1/Europe|Contenders Europe]]

===Format===
*Double Elimination
