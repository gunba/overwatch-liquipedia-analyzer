==Information==
Following the conclusion of the '''North America East''' and '''North America West''' Grand Finals, a tiebreaker match between the runner-ups of both Contenders will face off to qualify for the [[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Atlantic_Showdown|Atlantic Showdown]].

The result will also influence the seeding of the champions of both regions in the '''Atlantic Showdown'''.
