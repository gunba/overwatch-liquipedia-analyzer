==Information==
===Format===
*'''Regular Season:'''
**8 teams in a Round Robin format.
**[[Patch 1.36]]
** '''Top six (6)''' advance to the '''[[Overwatch_Contenders/2019/Season_2/Europe/Playoffs|Playoffs]]''':
***<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams</span> advances to the ''Semifinal''
***<span class="bg-stayup">Top three (3) to six (6)</span> advance to the  ''Quarterfinal''
*'''Playoffs:'''
**6 teams in a Single Elimination bracket.
**[[Patch 1.38]]
**Matches are First-to-3 except for the Grand Final, which is First-to-4.
**Higher seed picks first map. All further maps are picked by the previous loser. The other team picks sides.
**Teams are locked to 2 Damage, 2 Tank and 2 Support Heroes.
