===Format===
*16 teams in a '''Double Elimination''' format.
**Seeding based on '''2019 Contenders Results'''
** '''Top four (4)''' advance to '''[[Overwatch_Contenders/2020/Season_1/North America/Week_1|Contenders Week 1]]'''
** '''Five (5) thru Twelve (12)''' advance to Trials Week 1
** '''Bottom Two (2) ''' relegated to Open Division Playoffs
