==Information==
*The Gauntlet Asia will consist of 10 teams competing in group stages and ultimately a double-elimination bracket. 
*Top-performing APAC regions will send their teams from Season 2 to the Gauntlet.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage:''' (Round Robin)
**Eight of the ten teams are split in two '''groups of four'''.
***Group A will consist of: '''CN2, KR3, CN4, PAC'''
***Group B will consist of: '''KR2, CN3, KR4, OCE'''
****Champions of China and Korea will bypass Group Stage.
**Each group consists of first-to-three matches in a round-robin format.
**<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams</span> of each group advance to Knockout Stage.
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom two (2) teams</span> of each group are eliminated.
**All matches are first-to-3.
*'''Knockout Stage:''' (Double Elimination)
**'''6 teams''' (champions of China and Korea, as well as top two of each group) in a double elimination bracket.
**All matches are first-to-3, except for Grand Finals, which are first-to-4.
