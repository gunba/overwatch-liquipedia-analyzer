==Information==
The Gauntlet North America consists of two stages:
*a qualifier tournament, open to all teams, in a similar fashion as the Open Division format except that it is limited to 256 teams;
*a Knockout stage for teams made of [[Overwatch_Contenders/2020/Season_2/North America/November|November Contenders]] participants as well as the four finalists of the open qualifier.
*additionally, two showmatches are planned on the last day of the tournament, opposing North America & [[Overwatch Contenders/2020/The Gauntlet/Europe|Europe]] Champions & Runner-ups.

===Format===
*'''Open Qualifier:''' (Single Elimination)
**Every match is played in FT3 format.
**<span class="bg-up">Top four (4) teams</span> are qualified for the Knockout stage.
*'''Knockout Stage:''' (Double Elimination)
**'''12 teams''' in a double elimination bracket.
**All eight participants of the [[Overwatch_Contenders/2020/Season_2/North America/November|November]] season of Contenders are automatically qualified for the Knockout stage.
**All matches are first-to-3, except for Grand Finals, which are first-to-4.
