==Information==
===Format===
*40-team single-elimination to determine top 8 teams
** 1st Round: 30-team Single Elimination advances to 18-team 2nd Round
** 2nd Round: 24-team Single Elimination advances to 12-team 3rd Round
** 3rd Round: 16-team Single Elimination advances to 8-team Main Event
*Eight-team double-elimination playoffs
*Top teams from [[Overwatch Contenders/2021/Season 2/Korea|2021 Season 2]] are invited as top seeds
*Top 32 teams from [[WDG/OVERWATCH_OPEN_TOURNAMENT/2022/Season_1|OPEN TOURNAMENT]] get early registration
