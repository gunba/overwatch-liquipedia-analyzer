===Format===
*'''Group Stage:'''
**12-team round-robin
**All matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**<span class="bg-up">Top 8</span> teams advance to playoffs
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom 4</span> teams drop to {{LeagueIconSmall/open division}} [[Open_Division/2023/Summer_Qualifier/Europe|Open Division Playoffs]]
*'''Playoffs:'''
**Double-elimination bracket
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}
**All other matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
