==About==

The '''Overwatch DACH Series''' is the official league of DACH eSports. Eight Teams representing regions from Germany, Austria and Switzerland will compete against each other.

Due to Covid-19 concerns the Playoffs, planned to be held at DoKomi 2020 in Düsseldorf, had to be moved online. As a result of the situation the prizepool had to be crowdfunded. As of May 10th, this accumulated to over 400€<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/dachesports/status/1259513810446147585|title=Spendenstand Prizepool|date=2020-05-10|publisher=DACH eSports}}</ref>.

==Format==

*'''Group Stage''':
**Each team will play every other team once in a '''Round Robin''' style league.
**Every match will be '''first to 3'''. The first map will be decided by the organizers before the match, every following map is the choice of the previous map's loser.
**Standings tiebreakers:
***Number of match wins
***Cumulative map differential
***Number of map wins
***Direct match result
*'''Playoffs''':
**The top four teams of the group stage will qualify for the '''finals'''.
**Playoffs will consist of a '''Single-Elimination Bracket'''.
**The Semifinals and the 3rd-place match will be '''first to 3''', the Grand Finale will be '''first to 4'''.
