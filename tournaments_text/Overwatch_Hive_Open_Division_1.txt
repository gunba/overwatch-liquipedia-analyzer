==Information==
===Format===
*40-team round-robin (5 divisions)
*Group Stage: Round Robin best of 5 (bo5).
*Top four (4) teams of each division advance to divisional playoffs.
*Playoffs: Single Elimination.
*Semifinals: Best of five (bo5).
*Finals: Best of seven (bo7).
