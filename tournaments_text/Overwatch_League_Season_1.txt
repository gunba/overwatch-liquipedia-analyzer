==Information==
===Format===
*'''Preseason''' - ''December 6th - December 9th, 2017'' <!--[[File:Overwatch Trophy.jpg|right|250px|Overwatch League Trophy]]-->
**The preseason kicked off with a series of live-streamed exhibition matches featuring 11 teams (Philidelphia fusion did not play in the preseason) at the Blizzard Arena in Los Angeles on December 6th - 9th.
*'''Regular Season''' - ''January 10th - June 16th, 2018''
**Matches in the Atlantic and Pacific Divisions played out live from the Blizzard Arena each Wednesday through Saturday. While the season was divided into four stages, wins and losses in every stage counted towards the teams' full-season records and seeding for the postseason. On the last Sunday of each stage, the top teams competed for hefty bonuses and the title of stage winner.
***Stage 1 - ''January 10th - February 10th, 2018'' <ref name="Stage 1 Tickets on Sale Now">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-us/news/21338592/stage-1-tickets-on-sale-now|title=Stage 1 Tickets on Sale Now|author=Blizzard Entertainment |date=2017-12-11}}</ref>
***Stage 2 - ''February 21st - March 24th, 2018''
***Stage 3 - ''April 4th - May 5th, 2018''
***Stage 4 - ''May 16th - June 16th, 2018''
*'''Postseason (Playoffs)''' - ''June 17th - July 28th, 2018''
**After Stage 4 finished, the top finishers in each division along with the next four teams in rankings regardless of division qualified for playoffs, which took place from July 11th – 21st. The top two finishers received a first-round bye. The Grand Final on July 27th – 28th determined the first Overwatch League champion team, claiming glory, the Overwatch League trophy, and a USD $1 million bonus.
*'''All-Star''' - ''August 25th - August 26th, 2018''
**The Overwatch League kicked back for a weekend of festivities as the community’s favorite players faced off in various challenges.

====Transfer Window====
The '''Transfer Window''' is defined as the period of time in which Teams can complete trades involving other Teams and/or sign new Players. The Transfer Window was the only time in which these changes could occur following October 30th, 2017. 
* The '''Free Agent Signing Period''' opened on ''January 22nd, 2018''.
* The '''Trade Window''' opened on ''February 11th, 2018''.
* Newly acquired players were to be added to a team’s active roster beginning ''February 21st, 2018'', the first day of Stage 2.
* The '''Transfer Window''' closed on ''April 3rd, 2018'', and rosters were lock for the remainder of the season and playoffs.<ref name="Transfer Window">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-gb/news/21498037/reloading-rosters|title=RELOADING ROSTERS|author=Emerald Gao|of=Overwatch League|date=2018-01-26 }}</ref>
