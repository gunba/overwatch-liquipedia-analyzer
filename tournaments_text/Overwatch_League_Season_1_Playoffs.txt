==Information==
The '''Quarterfinal''' and '''Semifinal''' series ran from July 11th to July 21st and were played in [https://esports.blizzard.com/en-us/blizzard-arena/los-angeles Blizzard Arena Los Angeles].  The '''Grand Final''' took place on July 27th and 28th at [http://www.barclayscenter.com/ Barclays Center] in New York City.

===Format===
* After Stage 4 of the regular season finished, the top finishers in each division along with the next four teams in rankings regardless of division qualified for playoffs, which took place during July 11th – 21th. The top two finishers received a first-round bye. The Grand Final on July 27th – 28th determined the first Overwatch League champion team, claiming glory, the Overwatch League trophy, and a USD $1 million bonus.
** [[Patch 1.25]]
*** Newly Reworked [[Symmetra]] will be disabled.
** The winner of each round of the Playoffs will be the Team that prevails in a '''Series''' against its opponent.
** Each '''Series''' is a best-of-three '''Matches'''.
*** Each Match is a best-of-five '''Maps'''.
** The first place Team from each Division will earn a first-round bye in the Playoffs.
** After the ''Quarterfinals'', the bracket is '''reseeded''' by Regular Season record.
