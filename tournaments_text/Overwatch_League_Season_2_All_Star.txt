==Information==
* All-Stars festivities kick off on ''Wednesday, May 15'', with a series of '''custom games''' pitting Atlantic and Pacific All-Stars against each other. Then on ''Thursday, May 16'', the Atlantic and Pacific squads compete in the official '''All-Star Game''' Powered by Intel to determine which division is best.
