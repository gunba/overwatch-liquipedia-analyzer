==Format==
* 13 teams are seeded into Play-Ins and Playoffs according to regular season results.
* '''Play-Ins''': Single Elimination
** Seeds ten (10) thorugh 13 are starting in Round 1.
** Two (2) winners of Round 1 and seed nine (9) face off against seeds six (6) through eight (8) in Round 2.
** Three (3) winners of Round 2 advance to Playoffs.
* '''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
** Seeds one (1) through five (5) and three winners of Play-Ins in a double elimination bracket.
** Winners of Winners' Finals and Losers' Finals advance to [[Overwatch League/Season 3/Playoffs|Grand Finals]].
* All matches are played first-to-three.
