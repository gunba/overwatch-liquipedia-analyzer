===Format===
{{#lst:Overwatch_League/Season_4|Format Regular Season}}

====Details====
* All four Tournaments:<section begin="Format Tournaments"/>
** Three weeks of '''Qualifiers'''.
*** Teams are split into the East and the West region.
*** Top six (West) or four (East) teams qualify for the Regional Knockouts.
** '''Regional Knockouts'''
*** Single elimination with six teams in the West and four teams in the East.
*** Top two from each region qualify for the Tournament Weekend.
** '''Tournament Weekend'''
*** Double elimination with four teams.
*** Champion earns three, runner-up earns two and third place earns one League Point.<section end="Format Tournaments"/>
<br>
<section begin="Format May Melee"/>
* '''May Melee''' ''April 16th - May 9th, 2021
** No Hero Pools.
<section end="Format May Melee"/>
<section begin="Format June Joust"/>
* '''June Joust''' ''May 21st - June 13th, 2021
** Hero Pools.
<section end="Format June Joust"/>
<section begin="Format Summer Showdown"/>
* '''Summer Showdown''' ''June 25th - July 18th, 2021
** No Hero Pools.
<section end="Format Summer Showdown"/>
<section begin="Format Countdown Cup"/>
* '''Countdown Cup''' ''July 30th - August 22nd, 2021
** Hero Pools.
<section end="Format Countdown Cup"/>
