==About==
'''Note:''' The East regional knockouts were supposed to be played at a [[Hangzhou Spark]] homestand, with the rest of the China-based teams ([[Chengdu Hunters]], [[Guangzhou Charge]], [[Los Angeles Valiant]] and [[Shanghai Dragons]]) competing on location. The Korea-based teams ([[New York Excelsior]], [[Philadelphia Fusion]] and [[Seoul Dynasty]]) would've played these matches remotely.<ref name="Live Events China">{{cite web|url=https://overwatchleague.com/en-us/news/live-events-returning-to-china-this-season|title=Live Events Returning to China This Season|publisher='''[[Overwatch League]]'''|date=2021-04-14}}</ref>However, due to the latest COVID-19 situation in China, [[Hangzhou Spark]] cancelled the homestand.<ref name="HZ Homestand Cancelled">{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/hangzhou_spark/status/1422437244158177281|title=Out of an abundance of caution due to the escalating COVID-19 situation, we are saddened to announce the cancellation of the Hangzhou Spark August 13-15 Homestand.|publisher='''[[Hangzhou Spark]]'''|date=2021-08-02}}</ref>
