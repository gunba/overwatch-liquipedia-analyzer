==Information==
===Format===
* Full season
** Four weeks of '''Qualifiers'''.
** Teams are split into the East and the West region.
** Top eight (West) or four (East) teams qualify for each tournament.

* Kickoff Clash and Summer Showdown<section begin="Format Tournaments"/>
** Double elimination brackets.
** Champion earns three, runner-up earns two and all other teams earn one League Point.<section end="Format Tournaments"/>

* Midseason Madness<section begin="Midseason"/>
** One global double elimination bracket.
** Champion earns four, runner-up earns three, third place earns two and all other teams earn one League Point.<section end="Midseason"/>

* Countdown Cup<section begin="Countdown"/>
** Serves as Playoffs play-ins.
** Teams placed 7-10 (West) and 4-6 (East) qualify.
** Double elimination brackets.
** Top two (West) and one (East) teams qualify for playoffs.<section end="Countdown"/>
