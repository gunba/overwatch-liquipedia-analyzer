==About==
On August 23rd, 2019, Monkey Bubble [https://twitter.com/monkey_ow/status/1164944282400231425 announced] the Overwatch MB Eurocup Fundraiser as a funding drive to support OWWC teams in the server region. 
They have donated £100 as an initial prize pool but viewers can add to that by donating and buying merchandise.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches will follow the Contenders/OWL format of 4-map series.
*'''Playoffs''': Single-elimination
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}

<br>
The prize pool will be distributed to participants in the following three ways:
* The matcherino prize pool which people can [https://matcherino.com/tournaments/18980 donate] to will go to the highest placed team that requires it. e.g. If the top 2 teams have already secured funding to go to World Cup, the 3rd placed team will receive it.
* Country-specific merchandise purchasable from the [https://monkeybubble.store/ Monkey Bubble Store] will go directly towards the team.
* 10% of all merchandise will also go towards the highest placed team that requires it.
