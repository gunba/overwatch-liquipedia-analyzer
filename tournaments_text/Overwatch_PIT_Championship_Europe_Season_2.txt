==About==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin.
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*** Tiebreaker is a Bo3 on Control, played directly after the tied map.
** Top 4 teams from each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
*'''Playoffs''': Doble Elimination.
** Quarterfinals, Semifinals and from losers' Round 1 to losers' Round 3 matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
