==Information==
An official Asia-Pacific Blizzard tournament based in Taiwan featuring professional Overwatch teams from Taiwan, Japan, Thailand, Hong Kong, Korea and Australia. This event is a Tournament Server competition with 3 twitch streams broadcasting live in Mandarin, English and Thai ([[Overwatch_Pacific_Championship/2017/Season_2#Streams|links below]]).

===Format===
*'''Competition Rules'''
**'''Regular Season''': Double Round-Robin
***Each of the 8 teams in OPC will play against each of the other teams a total of 2 times. Each match will be played in a best-of-five format ({{Abbr/Bo5}}).
**'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
***The '''top four (4) teams of regular season''' will advance to the playoffs that will follow a ''Single Elimination'' format. There are two rounds of playoffs over a two (2) weeks period, and each week will feature three (3) consecutive match days. Each match is a best-of-seven format ({{Abbr/Bo7}}). The team that takes two wins out of 3 match days will be the winner of a Playoff round.
****The first will play against the fourth ('''1 vs 4''') and the second against the third ('''2 vs 3''').
<br>
*'''Home/Away Team Rules'''
**'''Regular Season''': Double Round Robin
***Eight teams will go through double Round-Robin matches in which each team will fight against the same opponent two times, as Home Team one time and Away Team for another one time.
***In the best-of-five ({{Abbr/Bo5}}) matches, the Home Team has the advantage to decide the first, the third and the fifth maps and their attack/defense, while Away team can decide the second and the fourth.
***Each team will have a “Home Team’s Day” once during regular season, which means that team will have home advantage in two consecutive matches on that day. 
**'''Playoffs'''
***The team that achieved higher place finish in the regular season will have home-team advantage during the Playoffs. In the 3 match days of the Playoffs, this team will have home team advantage on Day 1 and Day 3, while the lower placed team will play as home team on Day 2.
***In the {{Abbr/Bo7}} matches, the Home Team can decide the first, third, fifth and seventh maps and their attack/defense, while the Away Team can select the second, fourth and sixth.
