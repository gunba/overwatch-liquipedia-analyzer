==Information==
===Format===
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**All matches are Bo5.
***[[Lijiang Tower]] will be the first map on every match.
***The same type of maps can not be selected for every four set.
***One map can only be selected once for every match.
***The last map must be a Control map,except [[Lijiang Tower]] in the first set.
**Top 4 advance to [[Overwatch_Premier_Series/2017/Summer|OWPS 2017 Summer]].
