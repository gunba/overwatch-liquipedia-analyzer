The '''Overwatch Rumble''' by ''Rivalcade'' is a $10,000 {{Abbr/USD}} online tournament, featuring 8 directly invited teams and 8 teams from the [[Overwatch_Rumble/April/Open Qualifier |Open Qualifier]].

==Format==
*'''Qualifiers''': Single Elimination
**Top 8 teams advance to '''Group Stage'''.
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
***If Lower Bracket team wins, bracket reset is used, using {{Abbr/Bo3}} as the deciding game.
