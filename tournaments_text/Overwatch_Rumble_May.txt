==Information==
The '''Overwatch Rumble''' by ''Rivalcade'' is a $5,000 {{Abbr/USD}} Monthly online tournament, featuring 8 directly invited teams and 8 teams from the [[Overwatch Rumble/May/Open Qualifier |Open Qualifier]].

===Format===
*'''Qualifiers''': Single Elimination
**Top 8 teams advance to '''Group Stage'''.
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.

===Map Pool===
* [[Hybrid|Hybrid Maps]]
**[[King's Row]] / [[Numbani]] / [[Hollywood]]
* [[Control|Control Maps]]
**[[Nepal]] / [[Lijiang Tower]]
* [[Escort|Escort Maps]]
**[[Watchpoint: Gibraltar]] / [[Dorado]]
* [[Assault|Assault Maps]]
**[[Volskaya Industries]]
