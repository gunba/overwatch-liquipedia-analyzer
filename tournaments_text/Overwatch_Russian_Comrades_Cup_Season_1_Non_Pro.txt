==About==
Any team can play in Russian Comrades Cup. There are three divisions in the Cup: Pro, Semi-Pro and Non-Pro.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are '''{{Abbr/Bo3}}'''.
*'''Playoffs''':
**''Quarterfinal'' and ''Semifinal'' are '''{{Abbr/Bo3}}''', while ''Grand Final'' is '''{{Abbr/Bo5}}'''.

====Map Picks====
*The order of map picks is set according to rounds: '''first map''' is always a '''control map''' and set by administration. The way teams '''pick next maps''' is described below:
**'''Team that won''' a map '''bans one of the five maps''' according to round rules;
**'''Team that lost''' a map '''picks next map''' from the rest of four maps;
**'''Winning''' team picks side.
