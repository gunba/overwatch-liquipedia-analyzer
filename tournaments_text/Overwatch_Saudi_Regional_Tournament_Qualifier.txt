==Information==
A regional tournament with teams from 9 different countries competing online with 8 teams qualifying to the final playoffs in Jeddah. This tournament is the first of its kind for Overwatch in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

* It was announced on twitter on May 17th, that the deadline to sign up was extended and that the tournament was being rescheduled.<ref name="rescheduled">[https://twitter.com/SAFEISKSA/status/997122338071117849 Tournament Rescheduled announcement]</ref>
** The tournament was announced to be rescheduled for August 27-30th, 2018, with Qualifications taking place from the 3rd to the 17th of August.<ref name="new dates">[https://twitter.com/SAFEISKSA/status/1004885256649039872 The new timetable of Overwatch Saudi Regional Tournament has been set]</ref>
