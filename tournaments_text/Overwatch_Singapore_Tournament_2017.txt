==About==
Logitech G presents the “Overwatch Singapore Tournament”, calling for all FPS teams in Singapore to battle in the first Overwatch tournament in 2017. Teams will vie for a total prize pool of SGD 5,100 and other prizes sponsored by Logitech Singapore.
===Format===
'''Open Qualifiers:'''
<br/> A total of 2 Open Qualifiers. Each qualifier is a best-of-3 Double Elimination bracket where the top 4 Singaporean Teams will qualify for the Finals.
<br/> 
'''Finals:'''
<br/> The top 4 teams from each qualifiers will advance into a Single Elimination LAN finals. The quarter-finals will be a best-of-3 with the rest of the matches being a best-of-5 from the semi-finals onwards.

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 3''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Escort]]/[[Hybrid]] map.

*'''Best of 5''':
**1st Set: [[Control]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Assault]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Escort]]/[[Hybrid]] map.
**4th Set: [[Assault]]/[[Control]] map (excluding map chosen in 1st & 2nd set).
**5th Set: [[Escort]]/[[Hybrid]] map (excluding map chosen in 3rd set).

*In the case of a draw, a sudden death {{Abbr/Bo1}} round will be played on a [[Control]] map.
