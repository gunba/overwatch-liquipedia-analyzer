===Format===
*'''General Rules''': Competitive Mode
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
**Round of 64 - Round of 32 are {{Abbr/Bo1}}.
**Round of 16 - Round of 8 are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**Semifinal and Grand Final are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
*'''Map selection''': The map pools for the qualifier tournaments have been preselected as displayed below. For the remaining games, the maps will be picked by loser’s choice from the map pool, with the winner selecting the starting side. Once a map has been played, it will be no longer available in the series.
