==Information==
===Format===
*'''Group Stage''' - ''October 12th - October 14th, 2018''
**All eight teams are divided into two groups where they play in a Round Robin format.
**All matches are '''4 Game Sets.'''
**Top two teams in each group advance to the playoffs.
**Bottom two teams in each group is eliminated.
*'''Playoffs''' - ''October 21th, 2018''
**Single-Elimination Bracket
**Semi Finals {{abbr/Bo3}}
**Grand Finals {{abbr/Bo5}}
