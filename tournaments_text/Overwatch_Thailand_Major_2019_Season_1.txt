==Information==
===Format===
* Group stage
** [[Patch 1.36]] – [[Patch 1.37|1.37]].
** All matches are Online.
** All matches are played in a four (4) maps format:
*** Map rotation (ordered from first to fourth map): [[Control]], [[Escort]], [[Assault]], [[Hybrid]].
*** If both teams tie a map, tiebreaker will '''not''' be played and the tie map will not be shown on the scoreboard.
*** If both teams tie the series (2-2), a fifth map will be played on ([[Control]]).
** Top 2 teams of each group proceed to playoff bracket.
* Playoffs
** [[Patch 1.37]].
** Single Elimination.
** All matches are Offline. Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo3}} and Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
