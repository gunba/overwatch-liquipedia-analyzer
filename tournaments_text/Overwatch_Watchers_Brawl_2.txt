==Information==
The '''Overwatch Watcher's Brawl II''' is the second tournament of the series played in February 2019 [https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/541310797922566184/550540811679301632/g3017.png], hosted by [https://twitter.com/TheWatchersOW The Watchers]. Matches ran from February 3rd - 27th, 2019. All matches are casted and uploaded to [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLCL2_wiOe4CE9OPMPsDQvSgchemL3hFqt The Watcher's Youtube] channel.

===Format===
* Double Elimination
* Matches are Best of Five ({{Abbr/Bo5}})
