==Information==
The '''Overwatch Watcher's Brawl III''' is the third tournament of the series played in March 2019 [https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/541310797922566184/561013070458257418/g3017.png], hosted by [https://twitter.com/TheWatchersOW The Watchers]. Matches ran from March 4th - 28th, 2019. All matches are casted and uploaded to [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLCL2_wiOe4CEY5QM0pmsf4h_MOb6zQaJo The Watcher's Youtube] channel.

===Format===
* Double Elimination
* Matches are Best of Five ({{Abbr/Bo5}})
