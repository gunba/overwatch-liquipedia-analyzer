==Information==
===Format===
* '''Group Stage''': 2 Stage Round-Robin
** Group Stages are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
** 4 Groups Top (2) teams per group advance to the Group Stage 2 where Top (2) teams per group advance to Playoffs
** Bottom teams are eliminated.
* '''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
** Semi-Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
** Finals are {{Abbr/Bo7}}
