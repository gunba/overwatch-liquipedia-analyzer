==Information==

===Format===
* '''Group Stage'''
** Double elimination bracket
* '''Playoff'''
** Single elimination bracket.
* All matches are {{Abbr/Bo5}}.
* In each match, the first map is random. Then the winner decides the map, the loser deciding the side.  
* The winner advances to the main event.
