==Information==
===Format===
*Up to 32 teams in a 8 Groups '''Round Robin''' format.
*Matches will be played in a '''First to 2''' format.
*Playoffs will be in a '''Double Elimination''' format.
*Playoffs will be played in a '''First to 3''' format.
*Grand Finals will be played in a '''First to 4''' format.
*First map is picked by the higher seeded team.
