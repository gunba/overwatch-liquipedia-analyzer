===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin
**3 groups for 15 teams.
**All matches are Bo3.
**Top 2 of each group advance to '''Playoffs'''.
**[[Team_Skadis_Gift/SKG|SKG]] advances to '''Playoffs''' directly as champion of [[Pioneer_Cup/Season_2|Season 2]].
**Last spot of '''Playoffs''' is determined by community votes to team which is on the bottom 3 of each group.
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination
