==Information==
*'''PolyLAN 29''' is a LAN event in Switzerland, offering tournaments in various esport titles. It is organised by students of the EFPL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne).
*'''Playoffs''': ''April 14<sup>th</sup> - April 18<sup>th</sup> 2017''
**Double elimination bracket
