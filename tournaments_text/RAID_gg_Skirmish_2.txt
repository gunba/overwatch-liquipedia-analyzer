==Information==
The '''RAID.gg Skirmish #2''' is the second tournament of a monthly series played in September 2018<ref>[https://battlefy.com/raid/raid-skirmish-week-5-overwatch/5b8408dc8c020e03c2d4cce7/info?infoTab=details RAID Skirmish Week 5]</ref>, hosted by [[RAID.gg|RAID Gaming]]. Participating teams are required to have 4/6 of their members to be Residents of Ireland or Northern Ireland.
