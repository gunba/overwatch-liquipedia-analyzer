==Information==

The '''RAID.gg Skirmish #5''' is the fifth tournament of the series played in March 2019<ref>[https://battlefy.com/raid/raid-skirmish-2019-overwatch-round-2/5c7803afb886010334320c71/info?infoTab=details RAID Skirmish 2019 - Overwatch Round 2]</ref>, hosted by [[RAID Skirmish|RAID Gaming]]. Participating teams must be made up of players within the following countries: All countries within the European Economic Area (the European Union, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein) and Switzerland
