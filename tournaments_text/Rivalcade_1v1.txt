'''Rivalcade Overwatch 1v1 Sponsored by Trevor May''' is a 1v1 tournament which was organised by Rivalcade and sponsored by Trevor May.

==Format== 
*Single elimination bracket
*1v1 Elimination - Mystery Heroes
*Each round consists of a single, Best-of-9 match on Watchpoint: Antarctica.

==Prize Pool==
$100 {{Abbr/USD}} total prizepool, $70 for 1st place and $30 for 2nd place.
