==About==
In Russian Overwatch Cup Pro division can play any team.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage''': Round Robin 
**All matches are '''Bo2'''.
**'''Top 3 advance to playoffs.
*'''Playoffs''':
**<span class="bg-up">Top one (1) team</span> advances to the ''Grand Final'';
**<span class="bg-stayup">Top two (2) and three (3)</span> advance to the ''Semifinal''.
**''Semifinal'' is '''Bo3''', while ''Grand Final'' is '''Bo5'''.
