==Information==

===Format===
*'''Qualifiers''': Swiss System
**Top 4 teams advance to the Main Event.
*'''Main Event''': Double Elimination
**8 teams
**Top 4 teams from the RWCC #3 are invited directly to the Main Event. 
**Map ban system
**All matches are '''{{Abbr/Bo3}}''' except for the Grand Finals.
**Grand Finals are '''{{Abbr/Bo5}}'''.
