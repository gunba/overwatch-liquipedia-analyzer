==Information==
*{{hero|Venture}} is ''not'' available for play.
===Format===
*'''Phase 1''' - ''May 11 - 20, 2024''
**10 teams in a Single Round-Robin format
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} advance to the '''Playoffs'''
**{{Bgcolortext|stayup|Remaining teams}} advance to '''Phase 2'''
**All matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
*'''Phase 2''' - ''May 22 - 28, 2024''
**8 teams in a Single Round-Robin format
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 4 teams}} advance to the '''Playoffs'''
**{{Bgcolortext|down|Remaining teams}} are eliminated
**All matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
*'''Playoffs''' - ''June 7 - 10, 2024''
**Two groups of 3 teams in a Single Round-Robin format
**{{Bgcolortext|up|Top 2 teams}} from each group advance to '''Bracket Stage'''
**{{Bgcolortext|down|Remaining teams}} are eliminated
**All matches ''(excl. Upper, Lower Bracket Finals and Grand Finals)'' are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**Upper, Lower Bracket Finals and Grand Finals are {{Abbr/Ft4}}
