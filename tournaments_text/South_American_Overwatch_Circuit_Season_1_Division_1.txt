==Information==
The '''South American Overwatch Circuit: Season 1 - Division 1''' is the first division for the first season for the community South American Overwatch Circuit, featuring the best teams in the region.

===Format===
*'''Group Stage #1''': Round Robin.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top two teams''' are promoted to Winner's Group.
**'''Two last placed teams''' are demoted to Loser's Group.
*'''Group Stage #2''': Round Robin.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
**'''Top four teams''' from Winner's Group are promoted to Playoffs.
**'''Last team''' from Loser's Group is demoted to [[South_American_Overwatch_Circuit/Season_2/Division_1|South American Overwatch Circuit: Season 2 - Division 2]]
**'''Last team''' from Loser's Group is relegated to the promotion tournament.
*'''Playoffs''': Single Elimination
