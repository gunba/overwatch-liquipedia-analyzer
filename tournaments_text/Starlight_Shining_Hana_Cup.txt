==Information==
'''Starlight Shining Hana Cup''' is a series women's overwatch tournament organized and mainly sponsored by OWL commentator [[Vivi]]. It aims to provide a stage for female overwatch players to show themselves, and arise more extensive attention to female players and teams. The tournament includes Team Events and Individual Events.

===Format===
*Team Events
** First round and 3rd place matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}. The final is {{Abbr/Bo5}}. 
** Single Elimination bracket
** The first map is [[Lijiang Tower]], the winner to select whether attach or defend, the loser to select the next map,
