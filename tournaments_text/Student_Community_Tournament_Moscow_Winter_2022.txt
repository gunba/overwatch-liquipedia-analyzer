'''EGoG Student Community Tournament''' is a tournament organized by Enthusiastic Group of Gamers.

==Information==
===Format===
* Matches are played both '''Online''' and '''Offline'''
* Only students of the Moscow universities are allowed to participate.
* Teams can only be formed from the students of the same university. Each university can have one team.
*'''Qualifiers:'''
**Swiss bracket
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}
**'''Top 2 teams''' from each group advance to the play-offs.
*'''Play-offs:'''
**Double Elimination bracket, with 6 teams
**All matches are {{abbr/Bo3}}, Grand-finals are {{abbr/Bo5}}
