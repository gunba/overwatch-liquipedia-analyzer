==Format==
* '''Round Robin groups'''
** 11 teams
** Two groups
** All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Best three teams from each group advances to the '''playoffs'''

* '''Playoffs'''
** Six teams
** Quarterfinals and Semifinals are {{Abbr/Bo3}}
** Grand Final is {{Abbr/Bo5}}
