==Format==
*'''Group Stage:''' (Round Robin)
**All ten teams are split in two groups.
**Both groups are a {{Abbr/Bo2}} Round Robin groups.
**<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams</span> of both groups advance to playoffs.
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom three (3) teams</span> of both groups are eliminated.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo2}}.
*'''Finals:''' (Single Elimination)
**'''4 teams''' in a single elimination bracket.
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.
