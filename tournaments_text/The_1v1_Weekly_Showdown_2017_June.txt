'''The 1v1 weekly showdown: Presented by Trevor May''' is a 1v1 tournament which was organised by Trevor May.

==Format== 
*Single elimination bracket
*1v1 Elimination - Mystery Heroes
*Each round consists of a single, Best-of-9 match on Watchpoint: Antarctica.
