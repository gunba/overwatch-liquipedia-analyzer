==Information==
===Description===
The Women of Overwatch is an Overwatch tournament for the Female and Non-binary community, organised by [https://www.deathblossoms.gg/ Death Blossoms]. There are two separate SR brackets for this tournament, '''[[The Women of Overwatch/Upper Bracket|Upper Bracket]]''' and '''[[The Women of Overwatch/Lower Bracket|Lower Bracket]]'''. Bracket assignments were determined using team average SR, which was calculated with player Career Highs from Season 10 until the date of the tournament (beginning of Season 15).

===Format===
*'''Playoffs''': Double Elimination 
**All matches are {{Abbr/Bo3}}.

===Map Pool===
*'''Best of 3''':
**1st Set: [[Escort]] map.
**2nd Set: [[Hybrid]] map.
**3rd Set: [[Control]] map.
* The higher SR team selects the first map, then the loser of the first map selects the second map, and the loser of the second map selects the third map. The team not selecting the map can choose sides.
* In the case of a draw, a sudden death round will be played on one randomly chosen [[Nepal]] map.
