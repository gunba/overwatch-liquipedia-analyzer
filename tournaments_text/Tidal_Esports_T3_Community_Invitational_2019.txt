'''Tidal Esports T3 Community Invitational''' is a community event organized by Tidal Esports in conjunction with coach [[Natter]].

==Information==
===Format===
* Randomized Double Elimination Tournament
** Matches are Bo3 with the exception of the semi-finals and finals which are Bo5
** Participating teams must be classified as Tier 3
