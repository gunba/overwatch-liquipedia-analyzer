==Information==
===Format===
* '''Qualifiers''':
** Eight qualifier matches with a single elimination, best of three bracket.
** Top two teams per qualifier progress to playoffs.
* '''Playoffs''':
** Double elimination, best of three bracket.

==Prize Pool==
The first place team, in addition to the cash prize, receives Vancouver Titans jerseys and can participate in a match against the Titans.
