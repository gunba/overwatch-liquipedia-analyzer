'''Toronto Defiant Holiday Bash''' is a tournament organized by [[Toronto Defiant]].

==Information==
===Format===
* '''General Rules'''
::*3 match seeding round on a single [[Control]] map
::*Eight eight-team double elimination groups
:::*Top two teams advance to playoffs
::*Eight-team double elimination playoffs
:::*{{Abbr/Bo3}} until Losers' Finals
:::*Grand Finals and Losers' Finals are {{Abbr/Bo5}}
