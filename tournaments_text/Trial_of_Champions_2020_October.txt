'''Trial of Champions''' is a tournament organized by Elo Hell Esports.

==Information==
===Format===
* Rounds of 64 and 32 are single elimination.
** All matches are first to three.
* All rounds thereafter are double elimination.
** All matches are first to three.
** Grand Finals are first to four.
