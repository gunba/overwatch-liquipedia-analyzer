The '''UGC Collegiate Fall Season Playoffs''' is a tournament organized by Ultimate Gaming Championship.

==Information==
* Top 3 qualifying teams from each division
* Top 6 Double Elimination Bracket (Top teams from each division will receive byes)
* Best-of-Five game (BO5) in Double Elimination Bracket
* Best-of-Seven games (BO7) Finals
