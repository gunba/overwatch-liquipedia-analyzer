==About==
* Hosted by the French structure [http://www.ogaming.tv/ O'Gaming.tv]
* Only for teams of six players with at least two French without replacements.
* 32 teams competed, 8 teams were invited : T for Tilt, Redskin, GamersOrigin, GoldRush diggers, Melty, and Misfits.
