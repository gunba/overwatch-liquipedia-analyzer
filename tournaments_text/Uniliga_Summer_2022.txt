==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.
*Teams finishing Top 6 in the Sommer '22 Season and Top 4 in the Sommer '22 Relegation are invited to the 1st League, if the roster does not change more than 50% of players.
*Teams may fight for the remaining slots in the 1st League in a Wildcard Tournament.
*All other teams will enter the 2nd League.
