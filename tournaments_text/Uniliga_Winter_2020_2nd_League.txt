==About==

===Information===
*All players must be students at a German university.
*Each team must have a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 12 players.

===Format===
*The teams are split into two groups, depending on location.
**The northernmost half of the teams plays in the Northern Division, the other half in the Southern Division.
*Each gorup plays in a round robin format.
**<span class="bg-up">Top four (4) teams from each group</span> are promoted to the [[Uniliga/Winter_2020/Relegation|relegation tournament]].
**Each match will be played in a four-game-set.
**The first map is determined by tournament administration.
**Following maps are decided by the loser of the previous map. The winner picks the side.
