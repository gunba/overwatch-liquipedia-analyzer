==Format==
*'''Group Stage:'''
*Double Round Robin over Nine (9) Game Days
**All matches are {{Abbr/Ft3}}
**<span class="bg-up">Top four (4) teams</span> qualify for the Promotion Battle in the Relegation Tournament.
**<span class="bg-stayup">Teams placed fifth (5) and sixth (6)</span> are eligible for participation in the next competition unless there’s a significant roster change (less than 50% of players retained).
**<span class="bg-down">Bottom teams</span> have to secure their spots through various methods such as the Wildcard Tournament or through Relegation battles with lower league teams.
**Teams participating should retain more than 50% of their players for automatic eligibility, or else they might have to secure their spots through the Wildcard Tournament.
