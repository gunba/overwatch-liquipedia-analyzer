==Information==
===Format===
*15 teams will compete in a single elimination Open Qualifier Bracket. The teams that place 1st and 2nd will proceed to an invitational bracket on August 16th to face off against 6 semi-pro teams.
*Matches will be played in a '''Best of 5''' format.
*The first map of each round will be randomly selected.
*Following the first map, the loser will choose the next map. The winner will then choose which side they will start on.
