The '''Will English IVitational''' is a tournament organized by Will English IV.

==Information==
===Format===
* '''General Rules'''
::*Competitive Play ruleset
::*Group Stage:
:::*Four teams of four groups in a round-robin format
::*<span class="bg-up">Top two (2) teams</span> per group advance to Challenger Knockout Stage. Bottom two (2) teams per group advance to Rookie Knockout Stage
:::*Challenger Knockout Stage participants will compete for the prize pool.
:::*Rookie Knockout Stage participants will not be eligible for the prize pool.
::*Group stage matches are {{abbr|Ft2|First to 2}}, Knockout Stage is {{abbr|Ft3|First to 3}} and Challenger Grand Finals are {{abbr|Ft4|First to 4}}
