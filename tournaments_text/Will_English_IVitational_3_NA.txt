The '''Will English IVitational 3 - NA''' is a tournament organized by Will English IV.

==Information==
===Format===
*Competitive Play ruleset
*Group Stage:
**Two eleven-team double-elimination groups to determine 8 playoff spots
*Playoffs:
**Contenders teams get an auto bye to the playoffs
**Single-elimination bracket
*All matches are {{abbr|Ft3|First to 3}}
*Grand Finals are {{abbr|Ft4|First to 4}}
