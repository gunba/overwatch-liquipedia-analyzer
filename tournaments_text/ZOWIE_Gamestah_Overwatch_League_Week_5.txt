The '''ZOWIE Gamestah Overwatch League''' is a weekly League open to all Oceanic Overwatch teams. Match nights take place on Mondays and Thursdays, with Open Qualifiers played on Mondays, and Play-offs on Thursdays. After 5 weeks, the eight teams at the top of the Leaderboard are invited to the League Finals series.
